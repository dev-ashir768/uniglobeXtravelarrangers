Leisure  /  Affordable long term stays
Long Stay vacations are increasingly popular with Canadians. Spending a longer time away and out of the cold is a great way to escape our winters and experience a different part of the world. What’s more, spending an extended period of time in a destination can be a great-value way to travel. Packages include everything you need to have a home away from home and you can choose to kick back and settle into local life, use it as a home base for exploring the surrounding area, or both! We offer Long Stay vacations around the world so reach out to our travel agents to plan your perfect trip.
