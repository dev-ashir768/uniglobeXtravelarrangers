About us

Uniglobe Carefree Travel Group, with a head office in Saskatoon and operations throughout Western Canada has been a leading travel agency for both business and leisure travellers since we opened our doors in 1989. Our locally owned and operated offices offer personalized service to each of our customers with a focus on value, product knowledge and professionalism. Between our experience and our technology we are able to meet any travel industry challenge we encounter. Our team has hundreds of years of combined professional travel experience. Wherever you’re dreaming of travelling to, there’s a good chance we’ve been there!

We love giving back and creating positive impacts in our community and throughout the world. Recently members of our team have spent time at the Saskatoon Friendship Inn, Saskatoon Food Bank and rebuilding an orphanage in Puerto Vallarta and a school in Cancun.

We are a family! Spending time together and celebrating each others accomplishments and milestones is a large part of what makes our team so strong.

We believe in diversity! We are a 100% Canadian owned, women led company who believes in equal employment opportunity for all.
