About Uniglobe Travel International

 

Uniglobe Travel International is a Canadian-owned global travel leader driven by a passion for travel and a commitment to exceptional service. With a strong foundation of local reliability and entrepreneurial spirit, Uniglobe agencies and partners specialize in corporate and leisure travel services, meetings and incentive programs, and much more—tailored specifically for small and mid-sized enterprises (SMEs).

For over 40 years, Uniglobe has been a trusted name in the travel industry, operating under a well-recognized brand, unified systems, and high service standards. Our world headquarters is in Vancouver, BC, Canada, and we proudly generate an annual system-wide sales volume of $4.5 billion.

 

With a global reach spanning 40+ countries across six continents, Uniglobe provides on-the-ground expertise and support while servicing clients in 100+ countries worldwide.

Our Vision

At Uniglobe, our vision is simple: To be the travel partner of choice for SMEs.

We are dedicated to driving our clients’ success through smarter, more efficient travel solutions. When our customers thrive, we thrive.

Our service commitment is built on a strong three-part foundation:

  • Elevated Personal Service – Delivering a seamless, stress-free travel experience
  • Unrivalled SME Travel Expertise – Providing tailored solutions for growing businesses
  • Local Ownership, Global Reach – The best of both worlds—personalized service with worldwide support

Why Choose Uniglobe Travel?

  • Decades of proven expertise
  • Global strength with local service
  • Customized solutions for SMEs
  • Unmatched industry knowledge and innovation

Let Uniglobe Travel be your trusted partner in navigating the world of business travel. Wherever business takes you, we’ll get you there—better.
