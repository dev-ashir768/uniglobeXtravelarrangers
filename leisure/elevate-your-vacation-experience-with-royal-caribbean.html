Elevate Your Vacation Experience with Royal Caribbean

Set sail on a longer voyage with Royal Caribbean and unlock an entirely new level of travel. Whether you're cruising for 7 nights or more, every extra day at sea is packed with unforgettable moments, breathtaking destinations, and luxurious onboard experiences.

Why Choose a Longer Cruise?

More days at sea mean more time to relax, indulge, and explore without the rush. Here’s what you can enjoy on an extended vacation aboard Royal Caribbean:

World-Class Dining

From globally inspired cuisine to specialty restaurants and casual bites, every meal is a new discovery. Savor gourmet dinners, fresh seafood, and poolside snacks, all day, every day.

Vitality Spa & Wellness

Unwind with indulgent spa treatments, massages, and fitness classes. From hot stone therapies to sunrise yoga on deck, longer cruises give you time to truly disconnect and recharge.

Spectacular Onboard Shows & Entertainment

Every evening brings a new performance! Enjoy Broadway-caliber musicals, ice-skating shows, aqua theater productions, live comedy, game shows, and more. On longer sailings, you’ll have time to catch it all.

Adventure Ocean & Kids' Zones

Keep the little ones entertained with award-winning kids’ programs, water parks, arcades, and supervised play areas, featuring age-specific activities so the whole family can enjoy their vacation, their way.

Why settle for standard when you can sail in style?

Bigger Cabins, Better Comfort: Upgrade your stay to balcony and suites and enjoy more spacious staterooms.

Enjoy your private outdoor space, ocean views, and upgraded amenities designed to maximize relaxation. Ideal for longer trips, balcony and suites give you room to breathe, unwind, and truly feel at home.

Balcony Cabin:

  • Crowd Fave: Our most popular option
  • Private balcony offering either an ocean view or neighborhood view, overlooking Central Park or Surfside.

Suites: 

  • Live the 'suite' life
  • Take luxury to the next level with exclusive experiences and inclusive amenities

 

Sample Itinerary:

Spend 7 nights of adventure during your cruise onboard the newest ship in the fleet: Star of the Seas. The newest Icon Class super-sequel Star of the Seas℠ has it all — so everyone in your crew can show "either-or" the door and have the time of their life. It’s mindblowing thrills, next level chill, bolder bonding, epic celebrations and wanderlusting exploration — all rolled into one weeklong getaway that’s about to go down in history as The World’s Best Family Vacation.
