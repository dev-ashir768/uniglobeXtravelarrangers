Churchill Awaits: Majestic Wildlife & Northern Wonders

Experience the Arctic wonder of Churchill, known as the “Polar Bear Capital of the World”, on a hotel-based adventure that promises thrilling polar bear encounters. This expedition offers the unique opportunity to observe these magnificent creatures from the comfort of a Tundra Buggy® while immersing yourself in the remote charm of Churchill, a picturesque Canadian community. With two full days dedicated to exploring the Churchill Wildlife Management Area (CWMA), you’ll witness polar bears awaiting the freeze of Hudson Bay, and plenty of opportunities to admire subarctic wildlife in their pristine natural habitat. Additionally, dive into the rich history and culture of Churchill by visiting its cultural museums, one-of-a-kind shops, and local restaurants serving delectable regional cuisine.

This adventure invites you to the heart of the Arctic, offering unforgettable polar bear viewing experiences, wildlife encounters, and a chance to explore the cultural treasures of this remote Canadian gem.

Itinerary

Please note that this is a sample itinerary and is subject to change due to unforeseen circumstances, such as weather or unexpected closures.

Day 1: Arrive in Winnipeg

Arrive in Winnipeg. Discover Canada Tours can arrange roundtrip flights and/or transportation for your group from anywhere in the world. Please contact us and one of our travel agents will be happy to assist you further

Check into your hotel and explore the amenities onsite

Get to meet your group and interpretive guide this evening with a meet and greet

Day 2: Winnipeg to Churchill

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are included today

Take a private flight to Churchill early in the morning with your group

Enjoy a guided tour of the town of Churchill

Visit the world-renowned Polar Bear International House and the Parks Canada Visitor Centre. Tour the centres to learn about polar bear research, climate change, and the fur trade history of Churchill

Check into your hotel for the next 3 nights

Day 3: Churchill Adventures

All meals are included in your package today

Embark on a full-day Tundra Buggy® adventure in the Churchill Wildlife Management Area today

This evening, take part in a cultural presentation from a local resident on the traditions, history, wildlife and lifestyle of Churchill

Return to your hotel at night

Day 4: Churchill Adventures

All meals are included today

Join a dog sled ride and interpretive program to learn about Métis culture and the history of dog sledding

Visit the Itsanitaq Museum to explore one of Canada’s finest Inuit collections

Stay overnight at the Churchill hotel

Day 5: Churchill to Winnipeg

Please contact an agent today to inquire about booking this tour for your group.

Explore the rest of our Churchill tours.
