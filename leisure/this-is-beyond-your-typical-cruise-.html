This is Beyond Your Typical Cruise

Embark on a spectacular 7-night Caribbean cruise aboard the luxurious Celebrity Beyond, sailing round-trip from Fort Lauderdale and visiting Puerto Plata (Dominican Republic), Charlotte Amalie (St. Thomas), and Basseterre (St. Kitts & Nevis). Designed to redefine modern cruising, Celebrity Beyond features cutting-edge design, including the cantilevered Magic Carpet, the open-air Rooftop Garden, and world-class cuisine across more than 30 dining and bar venues. After departing Fort Lauderdale, your first stop is Puerto Plata, where lush mountains meet colonial charm—explore the historic city center, ride the cable car to Mount Isabel, or relax on the beaches of Playa Dorada. In St. Thomas, discover duty-free shopping in Charlotte Amalie, visit the scenic viewpoints of Paradise Point, or take a ferry to nearby St. John for pristine beaches and nature trails. The final port is Basseterre, St. Kitts & Nevis, a blend of volcanic landscapes and colonial history—perfect for a scenic railway tour, rainforest hike, or beachside escape. With relaxing sea days to enjoy the ship’s upscale amenities, this journey on Celebrity Beyond offers an unforgettable balance of Caribbean adventure and refined comfort.
