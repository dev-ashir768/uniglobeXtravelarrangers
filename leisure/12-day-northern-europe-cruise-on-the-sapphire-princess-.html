12 Day Northern Europe Cruise on The Sapphire Princess

Exploring Northern Europe in the summer is like stepping into a storybook of coastal charm, vibrant culture, and rich history. Start in Copenhagen, where you can stroll the colorful Nyhavn waterfront, visit the fairytale Tivoli Gardens, or tour the royal Rosenborg Castle. In Skagen, Denmark’s northernmost point, see where the Baltic and North Seas meet and admire the town’s famous golden light captured by local artists. Oslo offers a blend of modern design and Nordic heritage—don’t miss the Viking Ship Museum or a walk along the Aker Brygge waterfront. In Gdansk, accessed via Gdynia, Poland, explore medieval streets, the impressive Neptune Fountain, and learn about the city’s shipyard history at the Solidarity Museum. Klaipeda, Lithuania’s port city, charms with its half-timbered architecture and access to the UNESCO-listed Curonian Spit. In Riga, Latvia, discover one of Europe’s most beautiful Art Nouveau districts, lively markets, and a UNESCO-protected Old Town. Tallinn, Estonia blends cobblestone streets, medieval towers, and cutting-edge tech culture—all wrapped in Baltic magic. In Helsinki, explore the unique rock-hewn Temppeliaukio Church, seaside saunas, and stylish Nordic design shops. Nynäshamn, your gateway to Stockholm, offers easy access to Sweden’s capital, where you can wander through the royal palace, Vasa Museum, or the charming streets of Gamla Stan. Finally, step back in time in Visby, a UNESCO World Heritage site on the island of Gotland, where medieval walls and rose-covered ruins make for a truly enchanting finale before returning to Copenhagen.
