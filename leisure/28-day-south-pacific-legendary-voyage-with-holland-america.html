28-Day South Pacific Legendary Voyage with Holland America

What are Legendary Voyages?

Holland America offers longer cruises that weave the ceremony and splendor of Grand Voyages into immersive 25- to 59-day itineraries, carefully crafted to ensure that the journey enriches the destination. Those sweeping journeys take you to top destinations in the world and UNESCO World Heritage sites. Itineraries rooted in Holland America's 150+ history. Experience the iconic, the undiscovered and everything in between.

 

 

If you are:

  • Healthcare Workers, First Responders & Medical Professionals
  • Active, Retired & Veteran Military Personnel
  • Active and Retired Teachers/Educators

You can even earn more with Holland America's Community Appreciation Offer

Your dedication and service inspires everyone, and Holland America would like to say thanks. Choose from more than 500 itineraries and receive an Onboard Credit of US$100 per stateroom when you book a new cruise with Holland America Line. 

*Important: Holland America will verify your eligibility and must this must be completed before the cruise is confirmed. For full details please contact your Uniglobe travel advisor

28-Day Islands of the South Pacific: Sydney to Auckland

Trace along Australia’s east coast en route to Papua New Guinea, the Solomon Islands, Vanuatu, Fiji and Tonga. Snorkel the Great Barrier Reef and visit local island villages on this incredible voyage.
