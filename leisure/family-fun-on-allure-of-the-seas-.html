Family Fun on Allure of The Seas

FEBRUARY 2027 ON THE MOST AWARDED SHIP IN THE WORLD!

No, that’s not a typo. Flights to some popular destinations for February 2026 are already selling out. We found a great deal on a cruise on the wonderful Allure of the Seas for the 2027 school break (in some parts of Canada)  and want you to take advantage of it!

Balcony Stateroom

Adults From $1,928 CAD | Kids From $232 CAD | Teens From $1,435 CAD

 

Get ready for an unforgettable vacation aboard the newly Amplified Allure of the Seas®—the most awarded cruise ship in the world!

From high-flying zip line adventures 10 decks up to splash-filled fun at Splashaway Bay℠, every deck brings a new thrill. Indulge in world-class dining, vibrant nightlife with more bars than any other ship, and nonstop entertainment that’s earned Allure top honors like Best Large Cruise Ship and Best for Entertainment.

Whether you're craving excitement or relaxation, Allure of the Seas® is your ticket to the ultimate getaway.

 

Your breathtaking ports of call: 

February 14, 2027 – Miami, Florida

February 15, 2027 – Day at Sea

February 16, 2027 – Day at Sea

February 17, 2027 - Philipsburg, St.Maarten

LIVE EACH MOMENT TO THE MAX

When you set out on adventure like this, every moment onboard is an opportunity to make brag-worthy memories. Whether you’re fueling up on delicious gourmet hog dogs at Dog House, catching high-flying stunts at the AquaTheater, or practicing your salsa steps on the dance floor at Boleros, this is one thrill-filled getaway you’ll talk about for years to come. 

PLAY WITH YOUR WHOLE FAMILY

The newly Amplified Allure of the Seas® offers something for everyone, from down-time done right in the adults-only Solarium to plummeting down the Ultimate Abyss℠ and hang-ten sessions on the FlowRider®*. Little ones will also find endless ways to have a blast with group activities and games at Adventure Ocean®. No matter what your age, thrills await around every corner.  

LET THE FLAVOR FEST BEGIN

Stunning destinations and scene stealing entertainment aren’t the only things to look forward to on your vacation — there’s an adventure for your taste buds, too. Like mouthwatering Italian dishes at Giovanni’s℠ Table, craveworthy Southern comfort food at The Mason Jar℠, and tender prime meat served in a classic steakhouse setting at Chops Grille℠. Make sure to bring your appetite. 

NIGHTS YOU'LL NEVER FORGET

Make it a night out at the theater and jive to all your favorite ABBA hits in Mamma Mia! Then belt out everything from show tunes to shower tunes karaoke-style at On Air Club. Or keep things low-key and lounge around with tropical tiki cocktails at Pesky Parrot℠ before salsa stepping all night to live Latin bands at Boleros.

 

These prices won’t last long! Call 1-800-565-6562 to lock in your winter 2027 holiday today! As a thank you for booking with Uniglobe Carefree we’ll even put a bottle of sparkling wine, two litres of bottled water, a fruit plate and a $50 stateroom credit on your room account to get your vacation started on the right foot!

*Cruise price is per person based on 2 adults and 1 child and 1 teen per stateroom. Cruise does not include airfare, recommended travel insurance or Uniglobe professional fee and is subject to change.
