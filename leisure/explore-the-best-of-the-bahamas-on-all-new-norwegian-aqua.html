Explore the Best of the Bahamas on All-new Norwegian Aqua

Once in a blue moon, you experience something unforgettable. On Norwegian Aqua™, the newest ship, you might find that a blue moon happens more often than you'd think. In St Thomas, greet the green sea turtles and colourful fish as you snorkel the turquoise waters of Turtle Cove. Tortola is the largest of more than 50 British Virgin Islands and its velvety white sand is legendary. Drive through the capital of Road Town for a glimpse of its culture and charm or hit the beaches, especially The Baths, a geographic wonder of mammoth-sized granite boulders.

 

The Newest Ship Now Sailing the Caribbean

Meet Norwegian Aqua, the latest evolution of Norwegian's innovative Prima Class. Elevate every part of your vacation with more expansive outdoor space and unobstructed scenic views. Enjoy more first-of-its-kind experiences like the all-new Aqua Slidecoaster. And feel right at home with a greater variety of spacious suites. With her Christening in 2025, Norwegian Aqua is the newest ship sailing the Caribbean. Let each new experience flow into the next on a ship as inspiring as the ocean itself. This is Norwegian Aqua™. Make New Waves - now sailing!

 

Make New Waves on Aqua Slidecoaster

Get ready for the wildest ride at sea with the Aqua Slidecoaster, the world's first hybrid rollercoaster and waterslide at sea! This two-person rider launches you and a friend into another dimension reaching rip-roaring high speeds. 

Experiences Beyond Expectation

Show off your old-school skills with retro arcade games at Aqua Game Zone, or get an epic game going at Glow Court, a high-tech LED sports floor. Discover endless enjoyment from bow to stern with a ship as dynamic and enchanting as the ocean itself.

First-Ever Prince Tribute Show at Sea

Relive the magic of Prince in a new, immersive concert experience aboard Norwegian Aqua. Revolution: A Celebration of Prince brings his legendary sound, electrifying energy, and bold spirit to the high seas in an hour-long tribute show. Sing, dance, and celebrate with the cast, or watch from your seats.

Splash Around the Expansive Aqua Park & Main Pool

It's time to make waves! Stretch all the way out on our expanded pool deck with a frozen drink in hand. Gaze into the horizon from two infinity hot tubs or the brand-new infinity edge pool. Or if you're taking the little ones with you, The Aqua Park is the perfect place to let them splash and play the day away!
