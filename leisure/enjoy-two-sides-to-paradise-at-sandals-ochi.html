ENJOY TWO SIDES TO PARADISE AT SANDALS OCHI

Fun Beach Vibes With Space to Recharge

Sandals Ochi embodies the whole range of what Jamaica has to offer. On one end, there’s the sunny beach club bubbling with energy. On the other, you’ll find serene Butler Villas nestled in a 100-acre garden. Whichever home base you choose, there’s dozens of bars and restaurants to check out for a change of pace, including a speakeasy (if you know how to find it). If golf is your thing, our Upton Estate Golf & Country Club is close by, and green fees are included.

At Sandals Ochi, 16 restaurants take you on a culinary journey with dishes from the Orient, Northern Italy, the Caribbean, and France. With exceptional service, romantic settings, and perfectly paired Robert Mondavi Twin Oaks® wines, every meal is a memorable experience.

In the water or on dry land, there’s something for everyone at Sandals Ochi. You can do as much as you want to do - even PADI® Certified Scuba Diving in spectacular waters - or absolutely nothing at all because it’s all included, all unlimited, all the time.

 

PADI® CERTIFIED SCUBA DIVING

Some of the world’s most renowned reefs, walls and wrecks are found in these extraordinary waters. Sandals offers one of the most comprehensive scuba diving programs anywhere - and it’s all included!

WATER SPORTS

Every water sport imaginable is included along with top-of-the-line equipment and professional instruction. Sandals includes a full range of water sports like:• Kayaks • Windsurfing • Snorkeling • Sailboats • Hobie Cats • Scuba Diving • Paddle Boarding • Kneeboarding • Glass Bottom Boat • Aquatrikes

LAND ACTIVITIES

From the sands to the courts, Sandals includes unlimited land sports so there are no limits to what you can play, when you can play including:• Table Tennis • Shuffleboard • Lawn Chess • Croquet • Basketball • Day & Night Tennis • Pickleball • Beach Volleyball • Board Games • Pool Tables • Bocce Ball • Squash • Horseshoes

FITNESS CENTRE

Stay fit while enjoying your time away! Whether it’s in one of our high-energy group fitness classes or cardio and weight-training in our top-of-the-line Life Fitness equipment, you will be sure to find the fun in fitness!

ENTERTAINMENT DAY & NIGHT

No one throws a party like Sandals because here every day is a celebration. From the slightly wild and exotic to the modern and lively, you’ll find parties around the beach, around the pool and around the clock.

THE CARIBBEAN’S FIRST SPEAKEASY

This is something everyone will be speaking about - the first speakeasy in the Caribbean. Just speak the secret password for old-fashioned cocktails and live entertainment in a so-retro-it’s-chic-again ambience that takes you back to the 1920s.

GREEN FEES INCLUDED

Upton Estate Golf & Country Club

Golf along an emerald fairway overlooking the sea at Upton Estate Golf & Country Club, where green fees are included. Golf Digest gave this Par-72 course a 4-star rating, and it’s easy to see why. Enjoy a driving range with a chipping and putting green, and complimentary transportation to and from the course. 

Optional - PGA GOLF LESSONS

Want to work on perfecting your swing or to improve your game? PGA lessons can help you with all aspects of your game. Sign up (at an additional cost) at the tour Desk or at the Club. Or join in on one of our complimentary PGA-certified clinics, held at the Club on Tuesdays and Wednesdays.
