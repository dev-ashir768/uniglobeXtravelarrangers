Jamaica in January

Welcome to Iberostar Waves Rose Hall Beach – an all-inclusive resort set on the picturesque shores of Montego Bay, Jamaica. Part of an exclusive beachfront complex with two sister properties, this resort is the perfect escape for relaxation, indulgence, and island adventure.

Guests can unwind with a luxurious treatment at SPA Sensations or explore the underwater world with dive certification courses at the Dressel Diver Center. Whether you’re seeking tranquility or a touch of excitement, you’ll find it here.  Cool off with a sweet treat from Happy Cones, or enjoy local flavors and fresh bites at the Chill Out Beach Grill, all just steps from the sand. For something a little more upscale enjoy a la carte dining or a visit to the buffet - there are a total of 5 restaurants and 6 bars on property.  

Jamaica is an island rich in natural beauty—from hidden beaches and vibrant coral reefs to cascading waterfalls and ancient caves. Whether you’re traveling with a partner or friends, you’ll be captivated by the magic that surrounds you.

 

THIS HOTEL ACTIVELY CARES FOR THE ENVIRONMENT

  • Single-use plastic free hotel (bring your own reusable straws!)
  • Responsible consumption of seafood
  • Purified water fountains – bring your own refillable water bottles or use the ones provided to fill up with safe clean water throughout the resort
