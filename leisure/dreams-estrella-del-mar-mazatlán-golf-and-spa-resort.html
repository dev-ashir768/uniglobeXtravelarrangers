Dreams Estrella del Mar Mazatlán Golf & Spa Resort

Experience the “Pearl of Mexico’s Pacific Coast” at Dreams Estrella del Mar Mazatlán Golf & Spa Resort. This hidden oasis offers an ideal location within the exclusive Estrella del Mar Complex, just 5.5 miles from Mazatlán International Airport, 35 minutes from the historic downtown and right next to a professional golf course designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr., all with the signature Unlimited-Luxury® all-inclusive concept. Dreams Estrella del Mar is an all-suite resort featuring 358 suites offering picturesque views of the Pacific Ocean’s sparkling sapphire waters or the pristine pools. Each suite features a furnished balcony or terrace, some offering direct access to a pool from the balcony. The Preferred Club offers an extra level of luxury with access to a private lounge, larger suites and more.


Gourmet cuisine and satisfying snacks are served without required reservations at eight dining options including four à la carte, a grill, a buffet and a café with ice cream booth. Five bars and lounges, including a swim-up bar, offer limitless classic cocktails and signature drinks created with international and domestic top- shelf spirits. Or, order from room service at any hour, day or night, for tasty in-suite meals and snacks.

Spend the day relaxing poolside or on the golden shores of the Pacific Ocean. Splash with the kids at the on- site waterpark. Soak up the sun while enjoying the cool, circling waters of the lazy river and then join the entertainment team for exciting activities and evening entertainment. Let the kids play the day away at the Explorer’s Club for Kids or Core Zone Teens Club with fun and friendly staff to help make their stay unforgettable. While the kids are entertained, sneak away for a relaxing and rejuvenating spa experience at the Dreams Spa® featuring world-class products and a variety of sublime treatments. The ideal Unlimited-Luxury® getaway awaits at Dreams Estrella del Mar Mazatlán Golf & Spa Resort.

 

Golf

Dreams Estrella del Mar is located next door to Estrella del Mar Golf course, designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr. and offers guests discounted green fees.

It is considered one of the best and most beautiful resort courses in Mexico. The par-72 course is lined with towering palm trees and native vegetation with 7,015 yards from the back of the trees. Featuring newly enlarged redesigned greens and additional tee boxes with six holes along the Pacific Ocean
