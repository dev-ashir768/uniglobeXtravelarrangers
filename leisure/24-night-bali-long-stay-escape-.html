24 Night Bali Long Stay Escape

Discover the ultimate tropical escape with this 24-night long stay in enchanting Bali, paired with a two-night stopover in vibrant Singapore. Immerse yourself in Bali’s tranquil beaches, lush landscapes, and rich culture as you unwind and explore.. Wander through charming villages, savour local cuisine, and rejuvenate your spirit with wellness retreats. Then, elevate your experience with a dynamic city break in Singapore, where futuristic architecture meets heritage neighborhoods. This extended journey promises the perfect blend of relaxation and cosmopolitan flair, leaving you with unforgettable memories.

INCLUSIONS

  • Round-trip flight to Singapore (if Flight & Hotel package option is chosen)
  • Round-trip economy class airfare between Singapore and Denpasar, Indonesia
  • Shared airport and sightseeing transfers
  • 26 nights in Superior accommodation (24 nights in Benoa, 2 nights in Singapore)
  • Daily breakfast according to itinerary

Bonus offers:

  • 1 dinner and 2 lunches in Bali (must be booked directly with the hotel’s reception)
  • One bottle of wine per room per stay in Bali
  • 10% laundry discount in Bali         
  • One full-day shared tour in Bali (to be booked directly with the local representative)
  • One half-day shared tour in Singapore (to be booked directly with the local representative)   
  • Hotel taxes, fees and service charges                                                                                          
  • Service of English-speaking local guide for your Singapore sightseeing tours                             
  • Entrance fee of the monuments as mentioned in the itinerary

Day 1: Depart Canada

Overnight flight to Singapore’s International Airport. 

Day 2: Welcome to Bali

Upon arrival at Singapore Changi Airport, you will need to clear customs and immigration. Afterward, proceed to collect your luggage and head to check-in for your connecting flight to Denpasar, the capital of Indonesia’s Bali Province.

Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport, a local representative will be waiting for you, holding a sign with your name. They will assist you with your hotel transfer in Benoa for check-in. The rest of the day is yours to enjoy at leisure.

Day 3 - 25: Bali

Experience the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure with a long stay in the captivating coastal paradise of Benoa, Bali. Nestled on the tranquil southern peninsula, Benoa is your gateway to endless leisure, where every day unfolds at your own pace. Indulge in sun-soaked mornings on pristine beaches, take refreshing dips in the turquoise waters, or immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture with leisurely strolls through charming markets. With countless water sports, world-class resorts, and breathtaking sunsets, your time in Benoa promises pure bliss, allowing you to unwind and recharge while savouring the tropical allure of Bali.

Day 26: Bali – Singapore

After breakfast and check-out, your private transfer will take you to Ngurah Rai International Airport in Denpasar for your flight to "The Garden City", Singapore.

Upon arrival at Changi Airport, clear customs and immigration. Once completed, proceed to the exit, where a local representative will be waiting with a sign displaying your name. They will escort you to your private hotel transfer for check-in in Singapore for check-in. The rest of the day is yours to enjoy at leisure.

Day 27: Singapore

After breakfast, a local guide will meet you in the hotel lobby to take you on a half-day city tour (shared service), where you will explore the city’s most iconic landmarks.

After the tour, you will be transferred back to your hotel. The rest of the day is at your leisure.

 

 

Tijili Hotel Benoa is a vibrant four-star boutique resort tucked along the beachfront of Tanjung Benoa, Bali—an ideal blend of hip, artistic design and Balinese tradition . With 270 thoughtfully styled rooms—ranging from cozy courtyard balconies to sea‑view terraces—each features handcrafted Java-tiled floors and splashes of ethnic patterns that evoke local artistry with modern flair .

Guests enjoy direct beach access plus multiple pools, including a rooftop pool with panoramic ocean views, and a lively beach bar atmosphere at Warung Bamboo or Kakatua Restaurant serving tropical-inspired fare and cocktails . The in-house wellness offerings include Tijili Spa with Indonesian holistic treatments, a fitness center, and easy access to watersports like jet-skiing, parasailing, and snorkeling right on the resort’s stretch of coastline.
