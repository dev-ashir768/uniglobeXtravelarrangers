    Great Resorts of the Canadian Rockies

    Just because the Canadian Rockies is home to some of the most rugged terrain in North America doesn ’t mean you have to rough it! Under brilliantly lit skies, you ’ll relish three of the region ’s most coveted five-star resorts on this pampering tour of the Canadian Rockies. With overnight stays at the renowned Fairmont Jasper Park Lodge, Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, and Fairmont Banff Springs Hotel, also known as the “Castle of the Rockies,”you can expect the royal treatment. Majestic views await as you enjoy a guided walk along the turquoise waters of Lake Louise, a peaceful cruise on the gleaming Maligne Lake, and a thrilling Ice Explorer ride through the vast Columbia Icefield –while exceptional creature comforts welcome you back from the wilderness. Balancing the great outdoors with the grand indoors, this Canadian Rockies vacation is the perfect marriage of peaks and pools, conifers and concierge, and sparkling lakes and spas.

    Itinerary

    Day 1 - Arrive in Clagary, Alberta

    Stetsons and Skyscrapers

    Welcome to Calgary, where cowboys still ride tall in the saddle below the skyline of Alberta ’s “Cow Town.”At 6 pm, meet your Tour Director and travel companions for a welcome drink.

    Day 2 - Calgary - Lake Louise

    City Slickers and Sapphire Shores

    Take an orientation drive through cosmopolitan Calgary before heading to Lake Louise. Upon arrival, take a leisurely   guided heritage walk along the legendary lakeshore. Free time this afternoon and evening at the exclusive Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise.

    • Local Favorite
      WILDERNESS IN WATERCOLOR Explore the breathtaking shores of Lake Louise where its bright blue surface mirrors the sky between Banff ’s high peaks. Hear the storied history-from the early Stoney Nakoda First Nations people who named it “lake of the little fishes ”to the bold railroad barons of the Canadian Pacific Railway. Learn fascinating stories of frontiersmen and First Nations who first discovered the exquisite waters of Lake Louise.

    • Tour Highlight
      ACCOMMODATION SPOTLIGHT Named for a Victorian princess, Lake Louise is the perfect setting for the architecture, amenities, and atmosphere of the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise-designed to pamper the heartiest of alpine adventurers. Slip into the indoor pool and hot tub, soothe the soul at the fitness center and spa, hike in the lush green forest, canoe on Lake Louise ’s famous turquoise blue water or enjoy horseback riding on a nearby trail. With attention to your comfort and enjoyment amid the rugged Rocky Mountains, you ’ll enjoy concierge service, gift shops plus a variety of restaurants and activities.

    Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

    Day 3 - Lake Louise - Jasper

    Natural Wonders in the Rockies

    Scenic drive on Icefields Parkway reveals spectacular vistas, including the picturesque Peyto Lake (weather permitting). Drive through Banff ’s mountain cathedrals that tower above Natural Bridge, Kicking Horse River, and Emerald Lake. Next stop, Jasper National Park, a Dark Sky Preserve and the largest national park in the Canadian Rockies. Enjoy free time this afternoon.

    • Tour Highlight
      PICTURE PERFECT Nestled between the pines and peaks, see for yourself why Peyto Lake is one of the most photographed natural beauties in North America. This azure alpine lake strikes the pose as a model of perfection, delivering a visual feast you must see to believe!

    Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

    Day 4 - Jasper. Excursion to Maligne Lake

    The Wilderness Spirit

    A scenic cruise on Maligne Lake to Spirit Island reveals the wild beauty of an alpine wonderland. Free time this afternoon and evening in Jasper National Park.

    • Tour Highlight
      NATURAL WONDERS Between the silver and green glacial peaks, experience the views from the largest natural lake in Jasper National Park on a cruise across the waters of Maligne Lake. Glide on the water to Spirit Island, one of the most photographed spots on Earth. Keep your camera ready for further chances to capture a photo of deer, moose, eagles, or bears in this Rocky Mountain paradise.

    Meals: Breakfast

    Day 5 - Jasper - Columbia Icefield - Banff

    Moving Mountains and Lifting Hearts

    Depart Jasper with a scenic drive on Icefields Parkway en route to Columbia Icefield. Explore how landscapes are shaped with an epic ride aboard the mighty Columbia Ice Explorer all-terrain vehicle. Continue through Banff National Park to discover breathtaking beauty beyond imagination. Free time this evening.

    • Tour Highlight
      EPIC RIDE Be moved by an expedition across the Athabasca Glacier. Traverse this massive glacier aboard the all-terrain Columbia Ice Explorer and walk on the surface where the Earth ’s geology is changing beneath your feet. Feel the force of nature ’s strength on an ancient glacier that still carves the face of the Rocky Mountains today.

    Meals: Breakfast

    Day 6 - Banff

    Peak Perfection and Royal Tradition

    Explore Banff National Park with a scenic drive around Tunnel Mountain and witness the wonder of Bow River Falls with views of the peaks of the Canadian Rockies. Later, take advantage of an optional helicopter flightseeing trip with dramatic aerial views of the Rocky Mountains and Banff, or stroll the town ’s colorful streets. Meet an   officer of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police to hear tales in true Mounties tradition. Farewell dinner this evening.

    • Local Favorite
      HEROES ON HORSEBACK As the song goes, Canadian Mounties “always get their man.”Since the early days of the Northwest Territory, the brave members of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police have patrolled the mountains of Alberta and beyond with dignified duty. While visiting Banff, visit with a Canadian Mountie as a tip of the signature wide-brimmed hat begins the tales of adventure including the tradition behind the famous red jackets and regulation jodhpurs.

    Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

    Day 7 - Banff - Calgary

    Back in the Saddle

    Depart Banff with a morning drive to Calgary. Your vacation ends at Calgary International Airport or the Calgary tour hotel for those with post-nights. Schedule flights to depart from Calgary International Airport after 2 pm

    Meals: Breakfast

     

    Don ’t miss your chance to experience the beauty and luxury of the Canadian Rockies!
