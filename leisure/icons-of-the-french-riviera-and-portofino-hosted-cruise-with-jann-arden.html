    Icons of the French Riviera and Portofino Hosted Cruise with Jann Arden

    Enjoy the luxurious yet relaxed atmosphere of the French Riviera, Italian Riviera and Monaco on this intimate Signature Yacht Cruise. Relax on golden beaches, enjoy long lunches at seaside bistros, browse luxurious boutiques and soak up the vibrant nightlife.

     

    Sail with One of  Canada ’s Most Beloved  Icons

    Jann Arden is a multi-platinum, award-winning singer, songwriter, actor, and bestselling author. With 19 Top Ten singles, 8 JUNO Awards, and a star on Canada 's Walk of Fame, she is one of the nation ’s most celebrated artists. Join her on this exclusive sailing and enjoy an exclusive private concert, photo opportunity, welcome reception and Q &A event.  

     

     

     

    Fall in love with one of the world ’s most famous coastlines, an iconic stretch of Mediterranean magic between Barcelona and Nice. Wander the ancient streets of Marseille, discover the bohemian art scene of Saint-Tropez, mingle with celebrities during the Cannes Film Festival, sample fresh seafood in Portofino and try your luck at the legendary Casino de Monte-Carlo. Sailing on board the ultra-luxury

     

    May 15, 2026 Barcelona, Spain

    Welcome to Spain and the vibrant city of Barcelona. Renowned for its stunning beaches, innovative cuisine, edgy art scene and buzzing nightlife, it attracts visitors from across the globe.   

    Your journey begins here as soon as you step on board Scenic Eclipse II. Be warmly welcomed by the crew before your butler shows you to your luxurious verandah suite. As the sun sets over Barcelona, join your fellow guests on the Sky Deck for a pre-dinner cocktail with stunning city views. 

    Please book your flight to arrive into Barcelona prior to 01:00 PM.

    Inclusions: Dinner

    May 16, 2026 Marseille, France

    The oldest city in France, Marseille dates back to 600BC. Today, it ’s a fast-paced, modern city brimming with multicultural flavours, and flanked by breathtaking natural scenery and golden beaches. This is a place where grit and grandeur meet, offering a fascinating insight into contemporary French culture. Explore its ancient streets and diverse neighbourhoods, marvel at iconic landmarks such as the Basilique Notre-Dame de la Garde and treat yourself to the freshest seafood and traditional delicacies. Marseille is also the gateway to the region of Provence, famous for its wine and cuisine.

    Inclusions: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

    May 17, 2026  Pampelonne Beach >St. Tropez, France

    Pampelonne Beach is a pristine stretch of coastline nestled along the French Riviera near Saint-Tropez. With its shimmering azure waters and soft golden sands, this is the epitome of Mediterranean paradise. Scenic Eclipse II will spend the morning anchored offshore, so you can bask in the warm sunshine as you relax on your beach towel or indulge in a refreshing swim in the crystal-clear sea. Explore the vibrant beach clubs and chic restaurants that line the shore, offering exquisite cuisine and stylish ambiance.

    This afternoon, you ’ll sail the short distance to Saint-Tropez. Once a sleepy fishing village, it was transformed into a luxury hotspot for international travellers in the 1950s. Today, it still retains all the glitz and glamour, alongside impossibly blue seas, stunning beaches and a bohemian art scene.  

    Scenic Eclipse II will be docked here overnight, so you can see Saint-Tropez come alive after dark. Join the beautiful people along the waterfront, listen out for the sounds of live music and keep an eye out for visiting celebrities.

    Inclusions: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

    May 18, 2026 Cannes, France

    Welcome to the cinematic capital of the world. We ’ll arrive right in the middle of the famous Cannes Film Festival, so expect to see movie stars and their mega yachts. Feel the excitement as you explore the picture-perfect streets exuding subtle movie magic.  

    Explore haute-couture boutiques, relax on striped sun loungers along the beach, or sip cocktails at chic beachside bars. There ’s plenty of history and culture to be found in this coastal enclave, but it ’s really a place to relax and soak up the champagne lifestyle that plays out alongside the sparkling Mediterranean every day.

    Inclusions: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

    May 19, 2026 Portofino >Santa Margherita, Italy

    You ’ll call at two neighbouring ports today, tucked into a quiet curve of the Ligurian Coast.  Portofino, one of Italy ’s most famous resort towns. Postcard-perfect and oozing Mediterranean charm, Portofino is a place to soak up la dolce vita. Brightly coloured buildings sit against crystal-clear water with lush pine-covered hills in the distance. Another late departure means there ’s plenty of time to explore the luxury boutiques, swim in the turquoise bay, dine on exquisitely fresh seafood and look out for celebrities at the exclusive bars set around the Piazzetta.

    Santa Margherita is one of the more relaxed towns along the Italian Riviera, a place where people still live and work rather than a spot designed for tourists. Pretty pastel buildings line the shore, hundreds of fishing boats sit in the marina, and the imposing 16th century castle looks out over it all. Wander the narrow streets this morning and feel immersed in history.

    Inclusions: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

    May 20, 2026 Monte Carlo, Monaco

    Monte Carlo is the epitome of luxury and elegance, nestled within the principality of Monaco. Renowned for its iconic Casino de Monte-Carlo, this glamorous enclave offers a wealth of indulgent experiences. Explore the opulent streets lined with designer boutiques and bask in the Mediterranean sun along the picturesque coastline. With its world-class entertainment, exclusive clubs, and prestigious events like the Monaco Grand Prix, Monte Carlo captivates visitors with its timeless allure and sophistication. Whether you 're seeking excitement at the casino or relaxation in the lap of luxury, Monte Carlo promises an unforgettable day. 

    Inclusions: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

    May 21, 2026 Nice, France

    As your Scenic Eclipse II journey draws to a close, bid farewell in the picturesque city of Nice, a renowned gem of the C ôte d ’Azur. After savouring a final delightful breakfast on board, it will be time to disembark and commence your onward journey or return home, carrying cherished memories with you.  

    Please book your flight to depart out of Nice after 12:00 PM.

    Inclusions: Breakfast
