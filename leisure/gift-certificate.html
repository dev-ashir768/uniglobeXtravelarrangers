Give the Gift of Travel

Wondering what to get the person who has everything?

While some people may feel gift cards can be impersonal, when it comes to travel, gift cards are the best option! Booking a trip for someone else can be challenging. Do you know where and when they want to go? Do you know for how long they can be away? Do you know the exact spelling of their name on their passport?

These are just some of the important factors when booking a trip for yourself or someone else. Once booked, changing the traveller's name, travel dates or the destination can be costly, and sometimes not even permitted. A Uniglobe Travel gift card is the perfect gift of travel!

Travel is about experiences and creating happy memories that will last a lifetime – what gift could be better? Travel is a great gift idea for any holiday season, weddings & honeymoons, birthdays, anniversaries, Mother's Day, graduation or any celebration. You can even use them for corporate incentives and client appreciation. With the Gift of Travel, you can never go wrong!

Gift Certificates

Give the perfect gift for anyone who travels and help create a lifetime of memories by contributing to their journey with a Uniglobe Travel Gift Certificate.

Gift Registry

Are you tying the knot? With a Uniglobe Travel Gift Certificate your loved ones can send you on an unforgettable start to your marriage with an amazing honeymoon.

 

Corporate Incentives

Want to reward your hardworking employees or thank your best clients with an incredible incentive? Ask how Uniglobe Travel Gift Certificates can work for your organization.

 

The possibilities are endless! With a Uniglobe Travel gift certificate your gift could be a flight, a cruise, a vacation package or even a bucket list trip like a safari! Gift certificates never expire and our experienced consultants will always provide the best in personalized service and vacation advice.

Our Gift Certificates can be used for any type of travel Uniglobe Travel sells and they make a great gift for that hard to buy for person on your list.

Contact your Uniglobe Travel consultant today!
