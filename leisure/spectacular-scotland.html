    Leisure   /  Spectacular Scotland

    Spectacular Scotland

    Experience the best of Scotland, from its dramatic Highlands and rugged islands to its rich cultural heritage, on a journey thoughtfully designed to support the environment, local communities and heritage preservation. With more MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experiences and sustainable partners than ever, this tour ensures your travels have a positive impact. Meet a descendant of Robert the Bruce at his ancestral home; visit the Balmoral Estate, a royal favorite of King Charles; and explore the revival of tartan at Prickly Thistle, Scotland ’s first B-Corp certified textile mill. Search for seals, whales and seabirds in the pristine waters off Dornoch Bay and the Isle of Mull, supporting conservation-focused local operators. Unwind in luxury at Cameron House on the edge of Loch Lomond, where you can glide across the loch on an elegant champagne cruise —all while embracing a new era of responsible, high-end travel.

    April - October 2026 Deaprtures

     

    Day 1  Welcome to Edinburgh

    Arrive in style with a private chauffeur to your luxurious hotel. This evening, dine on fine Scottish cuisine at Wedgwood The Restaurant, an intimate dining gem on Edinburgh ’s historic Royal Mile.

    Exceptional Dining

    Wedgwood Restaurant on the Royal Mile

    Celebrate the start of your journey at Wedgwood The Restaurant, an intimate fine-dining gem on Edinburgh ’s historic Royal Mile. On a MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience, savor a menu crafted from seasonal, locally sourced ingredients, including hand-foraged herbs, expertly prepared by chef Paul Wedgwood and his team. It ’s the perfect introduction to Scotland ’s rich culinary heritage. Your visit directly supports the United Nations ’Sustainable Development Goal 12: Sustainable consumption and production patterns.

     

     

    Day 2  Edinburgh

    Uncover the rich heritage of Scotland ’s capital alongside your Local Expert, strolling the iconic Royal Mile to the historic St Giles ’Cathedral. Step inside Edinburgh Castle, majestically perched atop Castle Rock. Later, enjoy time at leisure to wander through Princes Street Gardens, admire the Scott Monument, or explore the masterpieces of the Scottish National Gallery.

    Curated Experiences

    St Giles ’Cathedral

    Step inside Edinburgh ’s historic St Giles ’Cathedral, where your Local Expert reveals its stunning Gothic architecture and storied past. Marvel at the intricate Thistle Chapel, home to Scotland ’s oldest chivalric order, and soak in the grandeur of this sacred landmark at the heart of the Royal Mile.

    Edinburgh Castle Visit

    A Local Expert guides you around magnificent Edinburgh Castle, perched on a black basalt outcrop. Discover the site ’s 900-year history and take in the spectacular panoramic views over the city ’s most famous landmarks.

     

    Day 3  St Andrews and Perth

    Savor a morning of exclusivity at Broomhall House, where Lord Bruce welcomes you for coffee and shares his family ’s remarkable legacy. Immerse yourself in the charm of St Andrews, the birthplace of golf and home to one of the oldest universities in the English-speaking world —where Prince William and Kate Middleton ’s story began. As the day unfolds, arrive at the tranquil Murrayshall Country Estate, where impressive scenery and the stirring sound of a piper ’s welcome create a truly memorable arrival.

    Curated Experiences

    Scottish Piper

    Enjoy a time-honored Highland welcome as you are gracefully escorted through Murrayshall Country Estate ’s picturesque parkland by a Scottish piper, his stirring melodies setting the tone for an unforgettable arrival steeped in tradition and grandeur.

    Orientation of Town of St Andrews

    Stroll through the historic town of St Andrews, the birthplace of golf and home to one of the world ’s oldest universities. Discover its medieval charm, scenic coastline, and the famed setting where Prince William and Kate Middleton first met.

    VIP Visit with Lord Bruce of Broomhall House

    Enjoy a private audience with Lord Bruce at his ancestral home, Broomhall House. Over coffee, gain exclusive insight into his family ’s storied legacy, tracing back to Robert the Bruce, in this rare and privileged encounter with Scottish nobility.

    Day 4  Perth, Culloden and Dornoch

    Journey through the spectacular landscapes of Cairngorms National Park, where Scotland ’s natural beauty unfolds at every turn. Explore the grounds of Balmoral Castle with a dedicated expert, discovering the vistas beloved by the Royal Family. Or, visit Crathie Kirk, the church closely tied to royal tradition, before walking the historic battlefield of Culloden, where Bonnie Prince Charlie ’s fate was sealed. This evening, dine at the elegant Dornoch Station Hotel, taking in spectacular views of the Scottish coastline.

    Curated Experiences

    Journey Through the Spectacular Scenery of the Cairngorms National Park.

    Journey through the sensational landscapes of Cairngorms National Park, where rugged mountains, ancient forests, and glistening lochs showcase Scotland ’s wild beauty. Admire the ever-changing scenery as you travel through this unspoiled wilderness, a true Highland spectacle.

    Visit the Grounds of Balmoral Castle

    Wander the serene grounds of Balmoral Castle with a dedicated historian, uncovering the estate ’s rich wildlife and scenic beauty. Discover why this Highland retreat has been a cherished sanctuary for the Royal Family since 1852.

    Visit the Historical Battleground of Culloden

    Step onto the solemn moorland of Culloden, where the last major battle on British soil unfolded in 1746. Follow the battle lines, see the soldiers ’graves and pause at the memorial cairn, a tribute to those who fought and fell.

    Exceptional Dining

    Dine at the Beautiful Dornoch Station Hotel

    Experience the finest Highland flavors at The Golden Gorse, Dornoch Station Hotel ’s restaurant. With a focus on seasonal, locally sourced meat and seafood, each dish showcases the best of Scotland ’s natural larder.

     

     

    Day 5  Dornoch Sightseeing

    Personalize Your Day with a choice of experiences: refine your skills with a golf lesson or explore the grandeur of Dunrobin Castle, a Highland gem. Pause at Loch Fleet Nature Reserve for a scenic viewpoint before enjoying time at leisure —stroll along Dornoch ’s beach, visit its historic cathedral, or unwind in the tranquil surroundings.

    Personalise Your Day

    Today you can select your choice of the below experiences. Your Travel Concierge will discuss and confirm your preference on trip.

    Golf Lesson

    Hone your skills with a professional golf lesson at one of Scotland ’s legendary courses. Whether a beginner or seasoned player, refine your technique in a stunning Highland setting, perfecting your swing where the sport was born.

    Visit Dunrobin Castle

    Step into the grandeur of Dunrobin Castle, Scotland ’s northernmost great house. With its striking French ch âteau-inspired spires, lavish interiors and coastal gardens, this historic estate offers a fascinating glimpse into the legacy of the Dukes of Sutherland.

    Loch Fleet Nature Reserve

    Pause at Loch Fleet on your way to Dunrobin, where pine-fringed shores frame a tranquil tidal basin. Watch for seals basking on the sandbanks and wading birds feeding in the shallows, a serene glimpse into the Highlands ’natural beauty.

     

    Day 6  Loch Ness and Oban

    Experience a MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®experience at Prickly Thistle, where the revival of Highland tartan supports sustainable artisans and their craft. Journey along the legendary shores of Loch Ness before exploring the wondrous landscapes of Glencoe with a wildlife ranger. Conclude your day in Oban at the elegant Knipoch House Hotel, where sea-view rooms offer a serene retreat. 

    Curated Experiences

    Highland Tartan Demonstration

    Join a MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience to  Prickly Thistle, the only tartan weaving mill in the Scottish Highlands, where tradition and sustainability intertwine. Led by a passionate, women-run team, this workshop preserves ancestral weaving techniques on rare 20th-century shuttle looms. Learn how innovation and heritage shape each design, ensuring Highland tartan continues to thrive for generations to come. Advancing the United Nations ’Sustainable Development Goal 11: Sustainable Cities &Communities. 

    Visit Glencoe with a Wildlife Ranger

    Visit Glencoe, a breathtaking National Nature Reserve where conservation and heritage intertwine on a MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience. Guided by expert rangers, discover efforts to restore indigenous species and protect rare habitats, ensuring this dramatic Highland landscape thrives for generations. Advancing the United Nations ’Sustainable Development Goal 15: Life on Land. 

    Travel Along the Shores of Loch Ness

    Journey along the legendary shores of Loch Ness, Scotland ’s most mysterious and voluminous loch. As you take in the Highland scenery, ponder the tales of the elusive Nessie, a legend that has captivated visitors for centuries.

    Day 7  The Isle of Mull

    Explore the untamed beauty of the Isle of Mull, where dramatic coastlines and rolling hills create a haven for wildlife. Journey through this beautiful island, home to white-tailed eagles, otters and whales. Personalize Your Day by sampling fine whisky at Tobermory Distillery or take part in a MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience at the Whale and Dolphin Trust, supporting vital marine conservation efforts.

    Curated Experiences                 Personalise Your Day

    Visit the Isle of Mull

    Venture to the wild and unspoiled Isle of Mull, where rugged coastlines, rolling hills, and abundant wildlife define this Hebridean

    Sample Some of the Finest Whiskies at the Tobermory Distillery

    Discover the craft behind one of Scotland ’s finest whiskies at Tobermory Distillery. Learn about the time-honored distilling process before savoring a guided tasting of their distinctive single malts, each capturing the essence of the Isle of Mull.

    Visit the Whale and Dolphin Trust

    Step into the world of marine conservation with the Hebridean Whale and Dolphin Trust on the Isle of Mull on this MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience. Learn how their research informs vital protection measures for whales, dolphins and porpoises while engaging communities in stewardship. Spot marine life on your return ferry and contribute to ongoing conservation efforts. Advancing United Nations ’Sustainable Development Goal 14: Life Below Water. 

    Day 8  Oban to Glasgow

    Enjoy an exceptional Michelin-starred lunch , where inventive flavors take center stage in the heart of Scotland. Then, retreat to the shores of Loch Lomond for a luxurious two-night stay at Cameron House, a stunning spa resort offering relaxation against a backdrop of the beautiful Highlands.

    Exceptional Dining

    Michelin Lunch

    Savor Michelin-starred cuisine with Scotland 's finest ingredients.

     

     

    Day 9  Loch Lomond

    Personalize Your Day with an unforgettable experience on Loch Lomond. Take to the skies on a seaplane adventure, soaring over the loch ’s shimmering waters and dramatic landscapes, or indulge in a champagne cruise, gliding across the tranquil loch in style. Raise a glass to your incredible journey through Scotland over dinner, sharing wine and memories with newfound friends.

    Curated Experiences                 Exceptional Dining

    Loch Lomond Champagne Cruise

    Glide across the serene waters of Loch Lomond on a luxurious champagne cruise. Take in the stunning scenery of rolling hills, secluded islands and shimmering lochside views whilst sipping a glass of champagne, making for an unforgettable and indulgent experience.

    Celebration Dinner with Wine

    Raise a glass to your unforgettable journey through Scotland over dinner, reflecting on the incredible moments and scenic landscapes that have made this adventure extraordinary.

     

    Day 10  Farewell Scotland

    Bid a fond farewell to Scotland with a private transfer to meet your flight.

     

    Magnificent Stays

    Kimpton Charlotte Square - Edinburgh 2 Nights

    Set in Edinburgh ’s elegant New Town, Kimpton Charlotte Square Hotel is a stylish retreat blending Georgian heritage with modern sophistication. This vibrant city escape offers beautifully designed rooms, exquisite dining and a lively glass-roofed courtyard —perfect for unwinding in the heart of Scotland ’s capital. 

    Murrayshall Country Estate - Perth 1 Night  

     

    Set in the historic Perthshire countryside, Murrayshall is a refined retreat and once home to 1st Baron Lynedoch. This 400-year-old estate offers award-winning dining, 27 holes of parkland golf and a range of outdoor pursuits, all surrounded by breathtaking Scottish scenery.

    Dornoch Station Hotel - Dornoch 2 Nights  

     

    Experience the finest Highland flavors at The Golden Gorse, Dornoch Station Hotel ’s restaurant. With a focus on seasonal, locally sourced meat and seafood, each dish showcases the best of Scotland ’s natural larder. 

    Knipoch House Hotel - Oban 2 Nights  

    A tranquil retreat on the shores of Loch Feochan, Knipoch House blends modern luxury with country house charm. Unwind in stylish rooms with loch views, soak in the outdoor hot tub and savor locally inspired cuisine. End your evening by the log fire or with a dram from the bar ’s impressive whisky collection. 

    Cameron House - Loch Lomond 2 Nights  

    Set on the tranquil shores of Loch Lomond, Cameron House offers an elegant retreat in the heart of The Trossachs National Park. With exceptional dining, a championship golf course and a world-class spa, every moment is tailored for pure relaxation.

     


    If you are looking for a truly incredible way to experience Scotland, this is the tour for you.
