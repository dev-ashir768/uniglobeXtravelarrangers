    Dreams Cap Cana Resort and Spa

    Nestled on the pristine shores of Juanillo Beach, Dreams Cap Cana Resort &Spa is a luxurious escape set within the exclusive gated community of Cap Cana —just 15 minutes from Punta Cana International Airport. With its turquoise waters, powdery white sands, and calm waves, this hidden gem of the Dominican Republic provides the perfect setting for both relaxation and family-friendly fun.

    The resort features 519 elegantly designed rooms, including 40 spacious villas, each blending comfort and style. Guests can enjoy sparkling swimming pools, a beachfront bar, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a world-class spa offering saunas, steam rooms, and private treatment cabins.

    As part of the prestigious Dreams Resorts &Spas collection, Dreams Cap Cana offers the Unlimited-Luxury ®experience —where everything is included. From gourmet dining at 9 distinct restaurants to handcrafted cocktails at 5 lively bars and lounges, every detail is designed for indulgence.

    For those seeking adventure, the resort ’s prime location offers access to a wide range of experiences, including a protected marina, exciting excursions, and the famous Punta Espada Golf Course, designed by Jack Nicklaus. Guests can also enjoy 24-hour room service, daily refreshed mini-bars, and the unique Sip, Savor &See Dining Experience, which allows dining at nearby resorts.

    With its combination of world-class amenities, family-friendly activities, and romantic settings, Dreams Cap Cana Resort &Spa is the ideal destination for couples, families, and anyone dreaming of an unforgettable Caribbean getaway.
