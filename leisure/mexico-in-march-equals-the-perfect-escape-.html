    Mexico in March Equals The Perfect Escape

    The Grand Palladium Vallarta Resort and Spa is nestled on a lush tropical hillside overlooking the beautiful Bay of Banderas, set in front of 300 metres of private beach. Rooms feature Mexican d écor and are spread across 3 buildings winding through paths of tropical gardens filled with flora and fauna of the area. A variety of dining options and activities make this an ideal getaway for families, couples and friends.

    Please note: Since the Grand Palladium Vallarta Resort and Spa has been built on a hill, several ramps and stairs are found throughout the property. There is no elevator on site.
