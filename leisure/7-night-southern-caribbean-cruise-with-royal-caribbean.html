7 Night Southern Caribbean Cruise with Royal Caribbean

Max out memories in the Southern Caribbean on 7 nights of cruising adventure. Set sail from Cartagena, Colombia onboard Serenade of the Seas and head out to explore wow-worthy beaches and crystal-clear waters. Kick back and relax with your crew on this epic getaway.

 

 

7 Night Southern Caribbean Cruise

Ship: Serenade of the Seas

Date: Sunday, Jan 11th - Sunday, Jan 18th, 2026

From / To: Cartagena, Colombia

Price: *Cruise only *Taxes & fees included (prices and availability are subject to change)

Starting from CAD $2,026/person (CAD $4,050 Total/Room)

 

Itinerary: 

Day 1: Sun, Jan 11, 2026 Cartagena, Colombia

Nestled in the northern coast of Colombia, Cartagena de Indias boasts colorful architecture, beautiful beaches, and a lively culture as enchanting as the city itself. One of the best things to do in Cartagena is to stroll through the heart of the historical sector and catch scenic views from the Plaza de Bolivar. Or explore the underground passages of Castillo de San Felipe de Barajas. If you’re a beach lover, head to Playa Blanca and enjoy delicious local dishes like fresh fish, coconut rice and arepa de huevo. And remember, a vacation from Cartagena is not complete without dancing to the tropical rhythms of cumbia. Before you cruise, hop on a multicolored chiva (party bus) or mingle with locals at upbeat bars around the city.

Day 2: At sea

Day 3: Tue, Jan 13, 2026 Oranjestad, Aruba

Oranjestad is best known for its colorful Dutch Colonial ‘gingerbread’ houses painted in bright Caribbean hues, powdery white beaches and crystal-clear aquamarine water. Explore Aruba’s unique geological formations amid desert-like landscapes. Or discover aquatic wonders under the waves on a snorkeling excursion or a thrilling submarine ride. Sun-seekers can choose the beach that best suits their style, from the activity-filled De Palm Island to resort-studded Palm Beach, one of the island’s most glorious stretches of sand.

Day 4: At sea

Day 5: Thu, Jan 15, 2026 Willemstad, Curacao

Discover the colonial-era fortresses and other landmarks of Willemstad on a trolley ride before exploring more on your own. Go on a distillery tour to sample the island’s signature spirit, Blue Curaçao, or make some new marine friends at the Sea Aquarium. Enjoy the island’s stunning turquoise seas on a boat, snorkeling or just relaxing on the sands.

Day 6: At sea

Day 7: Sat, Jan 17, 2026 Colón, Panama

Colón is your portal to many quintessential Panama attractions. See the world-famous Panama Canal at the Gatun locks. Explore the Caribbean Sea coast at pristine beaches like Playa La Angosta. Or take the one-hour drive to Panama City, where you can walk among Colónial-era buildings in Casco Viejo or take in the local feel of vibrant Avenida Central.

Day 8: Sun, Jan 18, 2026 Cartagena, Colombia

 

Popular shore excursions to discover the destination

There are so many more ways to make your journey even more magical!

Colón, Panama:

Hear tales of old Panama City and learn about its modern cosmopolitan charms from an informative guide.

Willemstad, Curacao:

Discover stalagmites, stalactites, unique species and local lore inside the limestone cavern.

Cartagena, Colombia:

Discover the UNESCO listed old town and San Felipe fortress, taste the flavors of Cartagena in the local coffee shop

Oranjestad, Aruba: 

Discover the vibrant town of Palm Beach. Savor incredible Caribbean flavors at one of the beachfront restaurants
