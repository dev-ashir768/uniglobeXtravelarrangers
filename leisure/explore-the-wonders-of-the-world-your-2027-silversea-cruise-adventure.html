    Explore the Wonders of the World: Your 2027 Silversea Cruise Adventure

    Embark on an extraordinary voyage with Silversea Cruises. This journey aboard the luxurious Silver Dawn will take you across four captivating stages of the world cruise. Each part promises exceptional destinations, unparalleled luxury, and unforgettable experiences.

    About the Ship:

    SAIL INTO ULTRA-LUXURY ON SILVER DAWN

    The perfect ship to experience and unlock the beauty of the world like never before. Elegantly modern, exclusively intimate, Silver Dawn is one of the smallest and most luxurious ships to offer a World Cruise. Step into superlative spaces and savour Silversea ’s legendary ambience, highly personalised service, and exquisite dining venues –including S.A.L.T. which explores each destination through the lens of its food, with ever-changing menus to match. Whet your curiosity with an array of stylish bars, relax in panoramic lounges or escape to cosy corners. And for the deepest indulgence, there is our enviable Otium experience for heavenly levels of bliss throughout your voyage.

    Itinerary  

    Los Angeles to Papeete

    • Dates: January 08 –January 30, 2027

    • Duration: 22 days

    • Route: From the bustling shores of Los Angeles to the serene paradise of Papeete, Tahiti.

    • Highlights: Discover the vibrant culture of Hawaii, the pristine beaches of Bora Bora, and the turquoise lagoons of French Polynesia.

    Papeete to Lautoka

    • Dates : January 30 –February 11, 2027

    • Duration: 12 days

    • Route: Journey deeper into the South Pacific, ending in Lautoka, Fiji.

    • Highlights: Immerse yourself in the lush landscapes of Moorea, explore the untouched beauty of Rarotonga, and relax in the tranquil atmosphere of Lautoka, the "Sugar City "of Fiji.

    Lautoka to Brisbane

    • Dates: February 11 –February 22, 2027

    • Duration: 11 days

    • Route: From the islands of Fiji to the vibrant city of Brisbane, Australia.

    • Highlights: Experience the vibrant marine life of the Great Barrier Reef, the charm of Vanuatu ’s beaches, and the cosmopolitan allure of Brisbane.

    Brisbane to Singapore

    • Dates: February 22 –March 12, 2027

    • Duration: 18 days

    • Route: Sail from Australia to the bustling cultural hub of Singapore.

    • Highlights: Visit the remote beauty of Indonesia 's Komodo Island, explore the historic city of Semarang, and soak in the dynamic energy of Singapore.

    Why Choose Silversea?

    • Luxurious Accommodations: Elegant suites with private verandas and dedicated butler service.

    • World-Class Dining: Indulge in globally inspired cuisines, paired with premium wines.

    • Enriching Experiences: Enjoy cultural performances, expert-led lectures, and shore excursions tailored to each destination.

    • Exclusive Services: Personalized itineraries, all-inclusive amenities, and immersive S.A.L.T. (Sea and Land Taste) culinary experiences.

    Your journey aboard the Silver Dawn will be a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and cultural discovery. Whether exploring vibrant cities, untouched islands, or the vast beauty of the oceans, this cruise is designed to be the voyage of a lifetime.

    INTIMATE ONBOARD EXPERIENCES

    Every aspect of your World Cruise is carefully curated to ensure you can immerse yourself in a sense of exploration. From culinary discoveries and evening entertainments, to blissful spa moments of wellness and relaxation, our enrichments offer a world of incredible onboard, as well as ashore.
     

    Onboard Entertainment

    As you travel in utmost comfort, you ’ll experience an array of enriching cultural and leisure events as well as deep dives into the soul of each destination, making this voyage-of-a-lifetime an even more extraordinary experience.

    S.A.L.T. (Sea and Land Taste)

    Travel even deeper with our Sea And Land Taste programme. A unique culinary journey that adapts to the changing culture of every place you visit. Discover the flavours of the world as you sail westward across a world of flavours.

    OTIVM: Beyond Wellness

    Rooted in the ancient Roman philosophy that living well and being well aren ’t mutually exclusive, Otium redefines luxury pampering at sea by creating holistic moments of unprecedented comfort that will transform the way you relax.
