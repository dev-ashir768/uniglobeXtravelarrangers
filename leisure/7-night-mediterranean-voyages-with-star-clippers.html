7-Night Mediterranean Voyages with Star Clippers

Experience the elegance of a tall ship while discovering some of the Mediterranean’s most stunning ports and scenic coastlines. Explore charming Lerici, the island beauty of Portoferraio on Elba, the pristine Sant’Amanza Beach in Corsica, the medieval town of Bonifacio, and the glamour of Monaco and Nice. Every port offers unforgettable experiences, from sun-soaked beaches to historic streets and scenic harbors.

 

Mediterranean Voyage Offer:

Discover the magic of the Mediterranean aboard the majestic Royal Clipper on select 7-night voyages. Choose from stunning itineraries along the French Riviera and Corsica, the historic coasts of Croatia and Montenegro, or the breathtaking Ligurian Sea.

Now, before November 30th, 2025, when you book your next select 7-night voyage onboard Royal Clipper, you can enjoy the choice of:

  • $500 CAD onboard credit, OR
  • Get a $500 CAD air credit

Rates Starting From $3,160CAD per person

 

About Royal Clipper

Inspired by the tall ship Preussen, the Royal Clipper has the proud distinction of being the largest and only five-masted full-rigged sailing ship built since her predecessor was launched at the beginning of the last century.

For connoisseurs of sail cruising, the 134 metres Royal Clipper offers the ultimate sea-going experience, balancing the grandeur, adventure and tradition of sailing with the superb service, amenities and accommodations of a yacht.

Find your secret hideaway on one of the hidden balconies on eiter side of the bow. Or climb the mast (with safty harness provided) to one of the passenger look out 'crow's-nests', where you'll have the grandest view of all! Royal Clipper's interior spaces are just as dazzling.
