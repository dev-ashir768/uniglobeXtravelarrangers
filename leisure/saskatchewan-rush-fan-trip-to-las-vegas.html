Saskatchewan Rush Fan Trip to Las Vegas

Saskatchewan Rush Fan Trip to Las Vegas
 

Get ready for the ultimate fan experience as the Saskatchewan Rush take the floor in Las Vegas! Join fellow fans for an unforgettable getaway packed with lacrosse action, Vegas excitement, and exclusive Rush fun.

✈️ Trip Highlights

  • Round-trip flights from Saskatoon January 8–11, 2026

  • 3 nights’ accommodation at the iconic New York-New York Hotel & Casino on the Las Vegas Strip

  • Game night – January 9th, 2026: Cheer on the Rush as they take on the Las Vegas Desert Dogs. Seats behind the Rush team box.  Your ticket includes a concession voucher and exclusive meet and greet on the field post game!  

  • Exclusive 2-hour pre-game party at BrewDog Las Vegas, including great food, unlimited drinks, and fan energy before the big game

  • Rush swag included – show your team spirit in style!

Hosted by Gregger from 98COOL, bringing the good vibes and keeping the fun going all weekend long

🥍 The Ultimate Fan Getaway

This isn’t just a trip—it’s a Rush road experience like no other! Soak up the bright lights of Las Vegas, enjoy world-class entertainment, and stand proud with your fellow fans as we bring that Saskatchewan energy to the desert.

Price:  From $1,329 CAD per person 

Includes taxes & fees

Spaces are limited — don’t miss your chance to join the Rush in Vegas!
👉 Contact the Uniglobe Carefree Travel team to book your spot today and be part of the action!

 

Want more sports action?  There's an NHL game at the T-Mobile Arena on the 10th! 
Prefer to add in something more iconicaly Las Vegas?  Ask us about options for the shows, attractions and activities available that weekend!  We would love to help make this your ultimate Las Vegas escape.
