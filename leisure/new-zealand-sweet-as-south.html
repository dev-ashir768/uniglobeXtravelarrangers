    New Zealand: Sweet As South

    Experience the best of Southern  New Zealand! From Lake Ōhau to Queenstown to Christchurch - it 's an adventure you won 't want to end. Start off on a scenic flight over Mt. Cook then get ready to hit Queenstown, the adventure Capital of the World. Try bungy, or you can opt to take a stunning Milford Sound cruise. In Franz Josef you 'll hike a glacier before your tour ends in Christchurch.

     

     

    2025 and 2026 Departure Dates Starting From $1,885 CAD  

     

    Day 1 Christchurch to Lake Ōhau via Lake Tekapo

    Christchurch

    Welcome to Christchurch (Ōtautahi)! New Zealand 's South Island is the home to adventure, adrenaline and good times and we 're here to take it all on.

    Lake Tekapo

    We 'll stop off to see the intense milky blue waters of glacially-fed Lake Tekapo (Takapō). Here we 'll also get to enjoy an unforgettable Dark Sky experience, an interactive multimedia exhibition that mixes stargazing, science and traditional Māori astronomy, teaching us how the Māori people used to use stars to harvest food, and about the importance of reducing light pollution.

    Lake Ōhau

    Onto Lake Ōhau (Ōhau), with its glorious views of Mt. Cook. Yet another swoon-worthy location where you 'll have to pick that jaw up off the ground.

    Lake Ōhau Lodge (or similar)

    Dinner included

    INCLUDED EXPERIENCES

    Spot the Peaks of Mt Cook

    Looking out at the peaks of Mt Cook across the glacial waters of Lake Ōhau... It was once a dream, but today we 'll make it your reality. (You 're welcome.)

    See the Church of the Good Shepard

    See the church on the shores of Lake Tekapo with a backdrop of snow-capped peaks - and get that iconic Insta pic too!

    Marvel at the Epic NZ Landscapes

    We 'll stop at Lake Pukaki, Lake Tekapo and Geraldine... and watch happily on as NZ 's astonishing natural beauty takes your breath away.

    Visit the Dark Sky Experience

    The Dark Sky Experience combines Māori astronomy, storytelling and science together in one truly unique interactive display.

    Day 2 Lake Ōhau to Te Anau via Arrowtown and Kingston

    Lake Ōhau

    Another day, another natural wonder to discover.

    Arrowtown

    We make a quick stop for lunch in the historic gold mining town of Arrowtown (Kā Muriwai) before heading on.

    Kingston

    Our next stop today is Kingston (Takerehaka) for an authentic and important NZ farm experience. Where a charity helps local girls to learn practical farming skills and gain qualifications. Contiki makes a donation on behalf of every traveller who visits the farm, so you can help more NZ women and girls plough through barriers and get ahead in their ‘field ’just by turning up.

    Te Anau

    Arriving in the stunning lakeside town of Te Anau, why not opt to take a guided jet boat trip to Fiordland National Park. From Te Anau through Lake Manapouri, it 's a pretty exhilarating way to soak up the scenery. Prefer to keep you feet (or wheels) on dry land. You can also take a trip to the ethereal glow worm caves (woaahhh) or simply cycle the gorgeous trails through native bush.

    The Village Inn Hotel (or similar)

    Breakfast included

    INCLUDED EXPERIENCES

    Support Women in Agriculture MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience

    Today we ’ll visit a local farm in Te Anau to support the Fairlight Foundation, which works to advance women in Agriculture. We ’ll learn all about farming, as well as the Fairlight Foundation and the work that they do.

    Free-time add on

    Explore the Fiordland on a JetBoat

    Enjoy the thrill of jet boating down the majestic trout filled Waiau River, to the serene Lake Manapouri, step foot into ancient beach forests and learn all about this unique location among Fiordland National Park.

    Expected Amount NZ$ 189.00

    Free-time add on

    Visit Glowworm caves

    Take an evening cruise along Lake Te Anau to visit underground caves with a twisting network of limestone passages filled with sculpted rock, whirlpools &a roaring underground waterfall. Deep inside the caves, you will be taken by small boat into a silent hidden grotto inhabited by thousands of glowworms, unique to New Zealand.

    Expected Amount NZ$ 135.00

     

    Day 3 Te Anau to Queenstown via Milford Sound

    Te Anau

    It 's time to make tracks to the spectacular natural wonder of Milford Sound.

    Milford Sound

    Welcome to New Zealand 's most stunning attraction, with a magical combination of mountain peaks, forest-clad cliffs and deep dark waters. We take an included cruise of the fiord and experience the beauty of this pristine destination by boat, the best way to see Milford Sound (Piopiotahi) in all its glory.

    Queenstown

    Welcome to the adventure capital of the world - Queenstown (Tāhuna). Rest up tonight - the next few days are going to be BIG.

    Lylo (or similar)

    Breakfast included

    INCLUDED EXPERIENCES

    Cruise Milford Sound Fjord

    Cruise along the glassy waters and get the kind of view people write poems about. You 'll soon see why it 's a UNESCO World Heritage site.

    Queenstown

You might be the one trailing through wine country and exploring wine cellars and caves, or you may be that one that crams in a bungy jump and ziplining &still craves more. It 's your holiday - run wild as you see fit.

    Take your spirit of adventure a level above (quite literally) and fly over shores of Lake Whakatipu on a helicopter. It 's a view you don 't wanna miss. Please note: prices start from $330.

Grab your 1-day Ski Lift Pass (with the option to hire equipment, clothing, and getting some lessons too!).     Day 6 Queenstown

    Queenstown

    With our last day in adrenalin city edging closer, this is the time to push yourself to the limits and jump over any mental barriers you 've put up around yourself. Embrace the spirit of adventure and conquer those niggling fears once and for all. For those who did their dash yesterday, a day staying mellow and enjoying this beautiful region will prove more than sufficient. Tonight you can choose to jump on a gondola and to wine &dine atop of the highest restaurant in Queenstown, Skyline. What a way to end our time here! 

    Lylo (or similar)

    Breakfast included

    Free-time add on

    Take a Kayak Trip to Paradise with Funyaks

    In this full-day excursion, venture into the beautiful, untouched world known as ‘Paradise ’&made famous in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. Speed in with a jet boat ride &cruise out at your own pace in a canoe. Average standard of fitness required.

    NZ$ 499.00

    Free-time add on

    Skydive over Lake Wakatipu

    This is the perfect adventure activity for those adrenaline-junkies in the adventure capital of the world. Tandem skydive with a professional, experienced jump master &feel the rush of free fall before floating over Lake Wakitipu &the Remarkables mountain range (12,000 ft/3,657m).

    NZ$ 430.00

    With a cable car ride up to the Skyline restaurant, you can take in the stunning views of Queenstown &the surrounding mountains. Then, enjoy panoramas &the night lights of the town below as you feast on a large buffet meal &enjoy some live entertainment.

    NZ$ 142.00

     

    Day 7 Queenstown to Franz Josef via Wānaka and Haast

    Queenstown

    Time to return our adrenalin levels to normal and make tracks to the wild (and often wet) West Coast and it 's towering glaciers.

    Wānaka

    Enjoying our scenic drive we 'll stop for lunch in Wānaka, a pretty ski resort town situated on a lake front and dwarfed by mountains. This town is fast becoming one of NZ 's most popular spots, and a trip to the 'Dough Bin 'for a luscious pie should be high on the list today.

    Haast

    Our first piece of NZ eye-candy comes in the form of Haast Pass (Tioripatea), beautified by ancient rainforests, small glaciers and waterfalls. Note how the scenery changes as we roll away from Queenstown through the Southern Alps.

    Franz Josef

    Playing host tonight is the town of Franz Josef (Kā Roimata o Hine Hukatere), home to some of NZ 's finest natural beauty with bushlands and glaciers that almost reach the sea. Tomorrow we 'll be out and about making the most of it - who 's excited?

    Rainforest Retreat (or similar)

    Breakfast, Dinner included

    INCLUDED EXPERIENCES

    Discover Wānaka

    A lake town with views for days, surrounded by beech forests, alpine lakes, and wilderness glaciers.

    INCLUDED EXPERIENCES

    Drive through the Show-Stopping Haast Pass

    Ready for some incredible views? We 'll drive through the gorgeous mountains of the Gates of Haast and catch once-in-a-lifetime views of the river below. Seems like Mother nature does have a favourite!

     

     

    Day 8 Franz Josef

    Franz Josef

    This morning will be another big ol 'tick off the list as we take to the ice of Franz Josef Glacier. We 've got a heli ride over the glacier up for grabs, or the opportunity to carve your own Pounamu (jade) pendant, under the tuition of a Māori mastercraftsman. Pounamu is sacred to the Māori people, and this is a great way to learn about its cultural significance, while also creating a beautiful please of jewellery.

    NZ$ 330.00

    Free-time add on

    Travel to Franz Josef Glacier by Helicopter and Hike on a Glacier

    'One stop shopping 'with a short helicopter flight onto Franz Josef Glacier followed by two hours of exploring this spectacular natural wonder on foot. You 'll be fully kitted out on the ice as your Local Guide gives you the low-down on how the glacier was formed.

    NZ$ 799.00

    Free-time add on

    Make Your Own Pounamu Pendant

    Day 9 Franz Josef to Christchurch via Hokitika and Arthur 's Pass

    Franz Josef

    Armed with a new found appreciation for frozen water, we hop back on the coach &start making our way back to Christchurch.

    Hokitika

    In the gold rush town of Hokitika, we 'll meet with a local Māori guide who ’ll teach us all  about pounamu on a unique tour. We ’ll learn about the history of the illustrious green stones  (which the South Island is named after) and its connection with the Māori people. 

    Arthur 's Pass

    Take in the beautiful scenery as we wind our way across the highest and most spectacular mountain pass across the Southern Alps.

    Christchurch

    Arriving into Christchurch marks the end of our adventure (sob). NZ - you 've been real.

    Breakfast included

    INCLUDED EXPERIENCES

    Learn about Pounamu with a Local Māori Guide MAKE TRAVEL MATTER ®Experience

    The South Island is known for Pounamu (in fact, it ’s even named after the stuff.) Today in Hokitika, we ’ll meet with a local Māori Guide, who ’ll tell us all about the connection between the illustrious greenstone and the Māori people. After that, we ’ll go and check some out for ourselves.
