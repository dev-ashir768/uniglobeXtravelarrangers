Explore the British Isles on a Small Ship Expedition

Explore the British Isles with Aurora Expedition's Small Ship Program in 2027

Aurora Expeditions has recently released their 2027 Small Ship Cruise Program that travels to more destinations than ever before! 

Embark on a journey through time and tradition as you delve into the captivating narratives of Viking sagas, British legacies and Celtic connections. From past to present, and place to place, through the lives of remarkable individuals and everyday people, the following voyages offer an immersive exploration of the rich heritage that continues to shape the nations of Northern Europe.

 

Onboard, every sailing is enriched by Master Storytellers:  acclaimed historians, genealogists, art experts and more who bring destinations to life, while on shore, you will enjoy a selection of immersive “Your Choice” excursions allowing you to personalise each journey, while enrichment programs - from wine tastings to historical immersions - make every day a delight. 

Book your 2027 voyage now and take advantage of early bird savings of up to 25%* off eligible itineraries.

Plus, solo travelers can enjoy 10 dedicated solo cabins on every sailing, with no single supplement.

 

Sample Itinerary: 

Ireland & Scotland: Saints, Saviours and Secrets

This quintessentially Irish journey pursues many an Irish tale, from the pious to the irreverent, tracing the footsteps of saints, strugglers, saviours, and the unsung heroes and heroines who have shaped Ireland’s history. From the harrowing trails of famine-stricken families to the poignant exodus of the two million Irish who sought solace in the New World. From political prisoners who waged a battle for independence, to literary luminaries who captured the spirit of their homeland in timeless prose. 

Join this amazing journey as you revel in the vibrant narratives of the Irish who crafted the story of Ireland, with a book-end journey to the streets of Edinburgh for a taste of Scotland’s own saints, saviours and secrets. 

Date: 17 Apr 2027 - 30 Apr 2027

Ship: Greg Mortimer

Itinerary:

Day 1 Dublin, Ireland

Day 2 Dublin, Ireland

Day 3 Embark Belfast, Northern Ireland, UK (Port: Belfast)

Day 4 Londonderry~Derry, Northern Ireland, UK (Port: Londonderry~Derry)

Day 5 Killybegs and Slieve League Cliffs, Ireland (Port: Killybegs)

Day 6 Galway and Cliffs of Moher (Tender port: Galway)

Day 7 Dingle and the Skellig Islands (Tender port: Dingle)

Day 8 Bantry (Tender Port: Bantry)

Day 9 Cork (Port: Cork)

Day 10 Waterford (Port: Waterford)

Day 11 Isle of Man, UK (Port: Douglas)

Day 12 Disembark Troon, Scotland, UK (Port: Troon) - Edinburgh

Day 13 Edinburgh

Day 14 Depart Edinburgh

Highlights: 

  • Discover the saints, saviours, and everyday heroes who shaped Ireland, Northern Ireland, and Scotland
  • Walk the streets once trod by Oscar Wilde, James Joyce and George Bernard Shaw exploring Dublin’s vibrant cultural scene
  • Uncover secrets of Edinburgh’s Royal Mile, visit Edinburgh Castle, and delve into the city’s ghostly underground tunnels
  • Be inspired by political prisoners and freedom fighters, from Dublin’s Kilmainham Gaol to Scotland’s William Wallace Monument
  • Journey on a pilgrimage from St Patrick’s grave and the nuns of Kylemore Abbey, to the rock of Cashel and Robert the Bruce

 

Price from: USD $12,146 pp (twin share). 

Apart from the onboard accomodation and food, the price also includes:

  • Airport arrival and transfer to the group hotel on the first day, and transfer from the disembarkation port on the final day of your voyage package
  • Two night’s hotel accommodation, including breakfast, in Dublin on Days 1 and 2
  • Two night’s hotel accommodation, including breakfast, in Edinburgh on Days 12 and 13
  • Meals listed as included during shore excursions and land portions of the package
  • Onboard Wi-Fi
  • Captain’s Farewell reception including four-course dinner, house cocktails, house beer and wine, non-alcoholic beverages
  • Pre-voyage and post-voyage excursions as listed*
  • ‘Your Choice’ shore excursions as listed*
  • Enrichment experiences as listed, including Welcome and Farewell Events
  • Gratuities during excursions and enrichment program for local guides, drivers, venues and local sites
  • Port surcharges, permits, tender, pilot and landing fees
  • Complimentary access to onboard expedition doctor and medical clinic (initial consultation)
  • Presentations and guiding services provided by our Voyage Host, Aurora Expeditions Team and local operators
