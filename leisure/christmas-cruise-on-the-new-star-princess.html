Christmas Cruise on the NEW Star Princess

The brand-new Star Princess is the ultimate holiday getaway for multi-generational families looking to celebrate Christmas together in comfort and style. With spacious staterooms and suites designed to accommodate families of all sizes, everyone can stay close while still enjoying their own space. Kids and teens will love the dedicated youth programs and activities, while parents and grandparents can unwind at the spa, sip cocktails by the pool, or take in world-class entertainment. Best of all, the festive décor, holiday dining, and seasonal celebrations onboard create a magical atmosphere where traditions meet new memories. From relaxed days at sea to exciting port adventures, the Star Princess offers something for every age—making it the perfect setting for a Christmas everyone will remember.

Family-Friendly Features of the New Star Princess

Youth & Teen Clubs
• Firefly Park Kids Club (ages 3–7) for storytelling, crafts, and creative play.
• Neon Grove (ages 8–12), a fun hangout packed with games and activities.
• The Underground Teen Lounge (ages 13–17), a cool space just for teens to connect and relax.

Pools, Splash & Outdoor Fun
• Splash pad and family-friendly pools perfect for sunny days at sea.
• “The Dome” transforms from a bright pool area by day into a dazzling entertainment venue by night.
• Plenty of shaded spots where families can unwind together.

Comfortable Accommodations
• Connecting staterooms and balcony cabins make it easy for families to stay close while enjoying extra space.
• The luxurious Sanctuary Collection offers even larger suites and exclusive perks for those wanting a special experience.

Entertainment for All Ages
• Movies Under the Stars—watch family favorites poolside under the night sky.
• A wide variety of dining options, from casual buffets to interactive experiences like teppanyaki.

Sports & Active Fun
• SkyDeck Sports Court for basketball, pickleball, and more.
• Jogging track and outdoor activities perfect for staying active together.

Convenience & Peace of Mind
• Non-smoking public areas for a fresher onboard experience.
• Late-night babysitting so parents can enjoy a night out while kids are cared for.

The Star Princess is designed to bring families together with something special for every generation—making it the perfect setting for unforgettable holiday memories at sea.  Contact your Uniglobe Travel professional to book your trip today!
