Australia's East Coast by Campervan

There’s no better way to experience Australia’s iconic East Coast than by taking to the open road. The stretch between Brisbane and Sydney is one of the most scenic and adventure-packed routes in the country. 

You’ll start your journey in Brisbane, where you can enjoy a couple of days in the laid-back capital of Queensland. You’ll kick-start the road portion of your adventure once you pick up your vehicle, where you can head up to the artsy Sunshine Coast or to Noosa for the Eumundi Market. You can visit iconic destinations like Rainbow Beach or Bundaberg before making your way down towards Sydney. Heading south along the coast, you can explore Byron Bay, Coffs Harbour or Port Macquarie, until your route eventually leads to bustling Sydney. 

Travelling by campervan gives you the ultimate freedom to explore at your own pace. Whether you're a nature lover, a foodie or simply seeking a taste of the open road, this campervan trip promises a mix of adventure and relaxation—served with a good dose of Aussie spirit.

Fuel up, pack your essentials—your coastal journey awaits!

Day 1 - Arrival in Brisbane

Upon arrival in Brisbane, collect your luggage and make your way to your hotel.

Explore the city at your leisure. Enjoy walking through busy Queen Street or the leafy riverfront City Botanical Gardens. Brisbane City Hall offers historic photos in the Museum and skyline views from its 1930s Clock Tower.    

Spend an overnight in Mantra Brisbane Spring Hill or The Point Brisbane.

Day 2  - Brisbane

Enjoy another day taking in the landmarks of Brisbane or book scenic day trips to the Sunshine Coast or the Gold Coast for some excellent surfing.

Spend an overnight at Mantra Brisbane Spring Hill or The Point Brisbane or similar.

Days 3 - 15  - Explore Australia by Campervan

Today, you will make your way to the Campervan rental location to pick up your vehicle and check out of the hotel. 

You will begin your cruising in a campervan today and will have the vehicle with you for the next 11 nights /12 days.  

At the end of your journey, you will need to drop off the campervan in the Sydney area. 

Enjoy your unforgettable journey on the open road! 

Day 16  - Sydney

Today, you will drop off the campervan vehicle by noon at the rental depot location in the Sydney area.

After dropping off the vehicle, check into your airport hotel and enjoy your last day exploring Sydney. 

Spend an overnight at Novotel Sydney Airport, Holiday Inn Express Sydney Airport or similar.

Day 17  - Departure from Sydney

Make your way to the airport for your return flight to Canada.
