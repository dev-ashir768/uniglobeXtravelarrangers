Venture Beyond the Horizon with Silversea

Save Up to 40% on Polar Expeditions

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime journey to the ends of the Earth, from discovering indigenous communities in the Arctic to hiking snowcapped peaks in Antarctica, Silversea invites you to explore these extraordinary regions.

 

Reserve your suite by November 10th, 2025 and enjoy:

  • Up to 40% savings per suite
  • Reduced deposits from just 15%

Explore the polar regions in ultra-luxury aboard Silversea’s all-inclusive expedition cruises.

 

 

Polar Region Exploration Cruises:

Antarctica Luxury Cruises

From the Antarctic Peninsula and the South Shetland Islands to South Georgia and the Falkland Islands, Silversea's world class expedition fleet takes you deeper into the authentic beauty of this fabled part of the world in exceptional comfort.

Arctic and Greenland

Silversea Expeditions’ Arctic cruises are the true definition of transformative travel. From close encounters with diverse wildlife to unrivaled expedition knowledge, there is no doubt that Silversea's Arctic cruises put the amaze into amazing.

The small ship cruises offer comfort, unrivaled expedition knowledge and some of the most spectacular landscapes you’re ever likely to see. That could mean scanning the horizon for polar bears, taking a Zodiac® cruise to access an Inuit community or learning how to take the perfect iceberg photo. With Silversea, you will be kayaking the glacial Arctic seas by day and dining in superlative luxury by night.

Featured Voyages:

King George Island to King George Island

This is a beautiful voyage to a bucket-list destination: the Antarctic. Experience one of the least visited regions on Earth in the comfort and style of Silver Cloud. Bookended by King George Island, you’ll venture into Antarctic Sound and explore the Antarctic Peninsula in depth while learning about the iconic region with our expert guides. Experience blue-hued icebergs and glaciers in this vast icy wilderness and spot a dazzling array of wildlife.

 

Puerto Williams to King George Island

Unwrap the ultimate festive adventure - meeting curious penguins, cruising by gigantic slabs of turquoise ice, and crunching across Antarctica’s untouched snow. Sail the legendary Drake Passage one way to reach the White Continent. What a way to celebrate Christmas - exploring the Peninsula’s unique wildlife and icy beauty. Close out the year in the South Shetlands - islands rich with explorer-lore - where a trio of penguin species dot extinct volcanic slopes.

 

Tromsø to Longyearbyen

Escape the ordinary and journey into a pristine Arctic world of wildlife, towering mountains, and unforgettable adventures. Starting in Tromsø, sail to Nordkapp - Europe’s northernmost point - observing kittiwakes and puffins. Venture even farther north of the Arctic Circle for a fully immersive Svalbard exploration. The cruise expert guides reveal the secrets of this remote archipelago, leading you on wildlife-spotting adventures.

 

Nuuk (Godthab) to Reykjavik

The natural marvels of Greenland never fail to amaze. Immerse yourself in the famed scenery of the north with two weeks of adventure - kayaking through serene fjords, skipping across calm waters on Zodiacs and cruising among towering volcanoes. Discover coral-blue glaciers, cherry-red houses dwarfed by mountains, and giant mammals in ice-scattered waters. Your expedition concludes on Icelandic shores, exploring an island and the colourful capital, Reykjavík.
