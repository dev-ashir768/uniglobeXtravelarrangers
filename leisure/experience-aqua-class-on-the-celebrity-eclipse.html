    Experience Aqua Class on The Celebrity Eclipse

    Set sail on a 6-night tropical escape aboard the luxurious Celebrity Eclipse, departing from Fort Lauderdale on November 16, 2025. This sun-soaked itinerary takes you to three incredible destinations —Grand Cayman, Cozumel, and Nassau —each offering a mix of beach bliss, cultural discovery, and unforgettable excursions.

    Enjoy two relaxing days at sea to take full advantage of the ship ’s world-class amenities, including fine dining, rejuvenating spa treatments, and captivating entertainment. Whether you 're snorkeling with stingrays in Grand Cayman, exploring ancient Mayan ruins in Cozumel, or soaking up Bahamian charm in Nassau, this cruise is the perfect blend of adventure and self care in the Caribbean.

    Itinerary  

    Day  1 - Fort Lauderdale, Florida  

    Fort Lauderdale offers beaches, golf, shopping, and cruises —all just minutes from the port. Explore Las Olas Boulevard, historic sites, or take a boat tour of Millionaire ’s Row. Visit Flamingo Gardens or shop at Galleria and Sawgrass Mills. Enjoy fresh seafood, citrus treats, and key lime pie. Arrive 2 –3 hours before your 3:30 p.m. cruise departure to relax and enjoy the ship.

    Day 2 - At Sea

    While at sea, indulge in some well-deserved relaxation before your next adventure. Unwind by the pool, pamper yourself at the spa, savor exquisite cuisine, or discover the unique venues on board. Your next incredible destination awaits.

    Day  3 - George Town, Grand Cayman   

    The Cayman Islands blend upscale living with Caribbean charm —think white-sand beaches, coral reefs, and duty-free shopping. Snorkel the USS Kittiwake, swim with stingrays at Stingray City, and relax on Seven Mile Beach. Shop designer boutiques in George Town and savor local flavors like conch fritters, Caribbean curries, and rum cake.

    Day  4  - Cozumel, Mexico  

    Cozumel is a paradise for divers, snorkelers, and beach lovers. Explore vibrant reefs, swim with tropical fish, or relax on the beach at Playa Mia. Shop for silver and local crafts in San Miguel, or take a ferry to Playa del Carmen and visit the ancient ruins of Tulum. Enjoy fresh seafood, tacos, guacamole, and margaritas by the sea.

    Day  5 - Day at Sea  

    A day at sea in AquaClass on the Celebrity Eclipse is the ultimate in relaxation —think exclusive access to the serene Persian Garden spa and a long, leisurely lunch at Blu with ocean views and clean cuisine. With plush stateroom perks, wellness touches throughout the ship, and no agenda but your own, it ’s indulgence at its most effortless.

    Day  6 - Nassau, Bahamas

    Nassau blends bustling markets with stunning beaches. Just steps from port, explore the Straw Market, relax on white sands, or swim with dolphins and sharks. Take a ferry to Paradise Island to visit Atlantis Resort or unwind at the new Royal Beach Club with private cabanas and swim-up bars. Enjoy duty-free shopping, local crafts, fresh seafood, and signature rum drinks for a true Bahamian escape.
