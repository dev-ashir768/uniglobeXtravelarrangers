Iceland Cruise July 2026

Imagine unpacking just once, then sailing into the land of fire and ice aboard the Celebrity Silhouette this July — a 7-night Icelandic odyssey through Reykjavik, Seyðisfjörður, Akureyri, and Ísafjörður. From Reykjavik, you’ll soak up Iceland’s capital’s mix of volcanic landscapes, lively cafés, & modern Nordic design. In Seyðisfjörður, tucked into an emerald fjord, you’ll wander among colourful wooden homes and artistic enclaves, all with waterfalls and mountain views waking you in the morning. Akureyri brings the charm of a fishing community turned vibrant northern city: think botanical gardens, great local food, and an overnight stay so you can explore beyond the port—perhaps visit Goðafoss or the Lake Mývatn region. Then Ísafjörður, in the dramatic Westfjords, offers rugged fjords, rich maritime history, dramatic cliffs and wildlife, intimate towns, fresh seafood, and that sense of frontier.

What really makes the Silhouette & Celebrity stand out on this route is their seamless balance of adventure + comfort: expert shore excursions, immersive culture, beautifully curated dining, refined public spaces, and luxury touches at sea — plus built-in days at sea so you can recharge and reflect between ports. Whether it’s the view from your balcony, the service aboard, or the memories ashore, this cruise delivers Iceland like you’ve never seen before.
