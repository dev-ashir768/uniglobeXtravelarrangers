AIR CANADA DEBUTS EXCLUSIVE AIR CANADA SIGNATURE SUITE FOR PREMIUM INTERNATIONAL CUSTOMERS AT ITS TORONTO GLOBAL HUB

Air Canada unveiled the Air Canada Signature Suite for its top International Business customers at its Toronto Pearson global hub. The new suite is an exclusive airport retreat where eligible premium customers can dine à la carte at a complimentary, full-service restaurant from a menu created by acclaimed chef David Hawksworth, or enjoy hors d'œuvres, champagne, fine wines and cocktails in an intimate, Canadian-designed and decorated setting.

The Air Canada Signature Suite officially opens December 1, 2017 and is accessible to all full fare paying International Business Class customers, excluding upgrades and most point redemption programs. The 6,400-square-foot suite, conveniently located by Air Canada's international departure gates at Toronto Pearson Terminal 1, can accommodate up to 160 premium customers and is fully complimentary. It hosts a full-service restaurant offering à la carte dining from a Hawksworth-designed menu. The suite also features a cocktail lounge space with full service bar and a selection of hors d'œuvres and specialty snacks, a quiet sitting area and concierge service.

The new WestJet gift card is perfect for the holiday season.  

The WestJet gift card is available in both plastic and eGift formats. The plastic card comes in their signature teal colour and can be mailed to a recipient. The eGift cards are delivered by email and you can choose from a variety of holiday-themed designs. Whether it’s for a corporate incentive, recognition program within your organization, or simply a way to say thank you to your clients and employees, give the gift of travel with a WestJet gift card.

The great news? WestJet gift cards can be redeemed for bookings made by UNIGLOBE Travel.
