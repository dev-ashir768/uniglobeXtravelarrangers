Air Canada Updates

Standardizing check-in time

A reminder that effective September 4th, Air Canada updated their check-in policy to streamline the customer’s travel experience.  The check-in cutoff time is now 60 minutes across all sectors.

This change ensures a smoother, more efficient boarding process, giving customers peace of mind as they prepare for takeoff. Frequent fliers will be pleased, because it means upgrades will be processed 15 minutes earlier.

Complimentary 2nd bag to Nigeria and Kenya

Travellers to Nigeria and Kenya can now bring a 2nd complimentary bag on Standard, Flex and Comfort fares (no change to Basic and Latitude fares). Applies to travel between Canada and U.S., and Nigeria and Kenya. Tickets must be issued on/after September 4.

Aeroplan and Free Texting

Make sure your Uniglobe Travel Advisor has your Aeroplan number on file to add to your Air Canada reservations.   Having your Aeroplan number on your booking will ensure you have access to and can enjoy free text messaging on board all Air Canada Wi-Fi enabled aircraft. You can use Apple’s iMessage, Meta’s Messenger and WhatsApp, Messages by Google, WeChat, LINE, KakaoTalk, Telegram, Tencent QQ and Rakuten’s Viber.  Contact your Uniglobe Travel Advisor and make sure your profile is up-to-date. 

 

Porter In-Flight 

For over 17 years, Porter has been redefining economy air travel. As they continue to expand across North America, they are delivering an unmatched experience for travellers. These awards highlight our dedication to offering top-notch service and genuine hospitality to all their passengers.  

No Middle Seats

Porter offers choices when it comes to seat selection. And good news! There’s no middle seat, just an aisle or window.

Tasty Bites and Premium Sips

On every flight, Porter offers complimentary premium snacks, and a choice of beverages including free beer and wine served in real glassware. They also have delicious fresh food options available on flights over 2.5 hours aboard their E195-E2 jets. With complimentary drinks and snacks, you’ll actually enjoy Economy when you fly Porter.

Free, Fast WiFi

Onboard Porter's E195-E2 jets you can enjoy free, fast WiFi from any personal device from the moment you board until the moment you leave the aircraft. Powered by Viasat, no app download or registration is required. Passengers can view an ad to access 30 minutes of free WiFi-access or they can login to their VIPorter Account to receive uninterrupted access for the remainder of their flight.  Not a Porter VIP member, it's easy to sign up!

Not a VIPorter member?

Porter has made flying even more rewarding for you. From a Porter flight, to checked bags, to seat selection and more, you earn on every dollar you spend. Learn more about VIPorter.
