      Porter for Business

      Porter 's elevated onboard service makes business travel actually enjoyable, with complimentary beverages - including wine and beer served in real glassware - a selection of free premium snacks, and planes with no middle seats. Delicious, fresh meal options are included with the  PorterReserve all-inclusive fare - the easy, all-in-one fare option for business travel. 

      Travelling for business  is even better on Porter 's new jets, with free, fast-streaming WiFi for all passengers. Stay connected to the office, check emails, access documents or take some time to relax and catch up on movies and TV through your favourite streaming apps.

      Porter Airlines offers convenient travel solutions for corporations of all sizes, with a personal touch.

      What You Can Expect With Porter

      Free, Fast WiFi

      Onboard the E195-E2 jets you  can enjoy free, fast WiFi from any personal device from the moment you  board until the moment you leave the aircraft. Powered by Viasat, no app download or registration is required. Passengers can view an ad to access 30 minutes of free WiFi-access or they can login to their VIPorter Account to receive uninterrupted access for the remainder of their flight.

      Seating

      Porter offers choices when it comes to seat selection.. And good news! There 's no middle seat, just an aisle or window.

      PorterReserve

      Try the PorterReserve all-inclusive economy experience and don 't stress the details. Get baggage, travel flexibility, food, drinks and premium seat selection in one easy, affordable fare. And, you 'll enjoy dedicated check-in, expedited security and early boarding to make your day-of-travel even easier.

      Porter Passes - Fly More, Save More!

      Porter 's  prepaid flight credits make booking trips easier. Redeem them at your convenience, with full transferability (booklets of 20 or more), and no blackout periods.

      10, 20, 50, or 100 Flight Credits - Use credits between two destinations, within a zone, or anywhere Porter flies

      No Blackout Periods - Redeem all credits within one year, any day of the year

      No Surprises - Porter Passes include all taxes, fees, charges, and surcharges

      Fare Flexibility - Three fare classes with options and perks to match

      Unlock VIPorter perks before you fly - Earn travel perks before you fly with the purchase of any pass

      With VIPorter, you can redeem your points for flights and earn benefits faster than any other Canadian airline. Porter has  built a program that works for you!

      Air Canada baggage allowances are changing
      for select markets

      Air Canada is revising its baggage allowances for some fare options on select international markets.

      These changes deliver clearer choices between  fare options and give customers more value. 

      New - Comfort Fares on Asia-Pacific: 
      Comfort fares will be introduced on Asia-Pacific  routes by January 21, 2025, providing customers with two complimentary checked bags. These fares are fully refundable and changeable –only fare differences apply to changes. On longer journeys, the comfort that an extra bag brings matters. With this fare option, customers choose the fare that best meets their needs.

      Basic Economy:  
      For Basic Economy tickets issued on or after January 21, 2025, checked baggage fees will apply for all bags on Asia and South Pacific markets, consistent with Air Canada 's  other international routes. 

      Flex Fares: 
      For Flex tickets issued on or after January 21, 2025, a  second complimentary checked bag  will be included on flights between  Canada/U.S. and the Philippines.
        
      Baggage allowance will also increase to  two complimentary checked bags  to the following destinations  on tickets originating in Canada*: 

      Algeria

      Bangladesh

      Iran

      Jordan

      Mauritius

      Morocco

      Pakistan

      Saudi Arabia

      Sri Lanka

      Tunisia

      T ürkiye

      *If the ticket issued to the above does not originate in Canada, only one checked bag remains complimentary.

      For example:

      Montreal-Algiers-Montreal - Flex fare –two complimentary checked bags  

      Algiers-Montreal-Algiers - Flex fare –one complimentary checked bag  
       

      Use Air Canada 's  Baggage Calculator  to determine baggage allowances by route and fare type.
