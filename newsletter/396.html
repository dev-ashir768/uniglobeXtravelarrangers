      Our Technology Solutions

      Uniglobe's technology solutions offer you a more efficient method of managing your travel needs. They are designed to provide travellers control over their schedule and itinerary with the guarantee of quality, flexibility and speed.

      We offer a variety of technology programs that will provide you with instant solutions to many travel-related problems and situations as well as having the support of your Uniglobe Travel Advisor.  We are with you every step of the way with all of the resources and tools you'll need when we get back to travelling.

      Contact us to learn more about our services and self-serve tools, including:

      • E-Itinerary
      • Flight Tracking
      • Credit Tracking
      • Online Booking Tool
      • Quality Control & Reporting
      • Risk Managment 
      • Trip Start
      • Trip Request
      • Uniglobe App

      Uniglobe Travel is here to ensure you are safe and well taken care of before, during and after your trip.

      Up-to-date Information

      With the many travel protocols currently in place, we want to ensure you are informed with the most up to date health information.  We have created a Covid-19 section on our website for your use.  These pages give you the most current updates provided by our suppliers on travel restrictions and cleaning protocols.  If you have questions or need some advice, your Uniglobe Travel Advisor can help guide you through the information.

      When we get back to travelling and you are not sure if you require a Covid test prior to or upon arrival, use the link below to find a listing of PCR testing facilities by country.

      Go to https://travel.gc.ca/travelling/advisories

      Once you click the link above, scroll down and click on the country followed by that country’s Health tab. (Not all destinations have facilities listed)

      The well being and safety of our clients is our priority. When you are ready to book your next trip, our advisors can offer all the advice and expertise you’ll need for a wide range of booking options.
