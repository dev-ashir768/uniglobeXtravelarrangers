Your Partner Every Step of the Way

Think of Uniglobe Travel as your corporate travel partner as we emerge from this pandemic.  We can help you navigate a world that will include changing booking requirements and updated travel restrictions. This includes consulting on your travel policies and helping you make the right decisions for your company and your travellers while being your “go to” source for current information and resources.

Uniglobe’s Exceptional Care means our advisors manage your travel needs and mitigate your risks, ensuring your travellers are safe and well taken care of.  We also offer services and programs designed to protect you and your company such as Uniglobe TripCheck and our Duty of Care provisions for safety, security and tracking of travellers.

A Perfect Meeting Space For Every Occasion in the Caribbean

Hosting your next corporate event, conference or team-building activity at Casa de Campo will be completely worth the time for everyone involved.

Make paradise the location of your next event and experience luxury in the Caribbean while accomplishing team goals.  Casa de Campo offers a wide variety of scenarios to accommodate groups from 10 to 6,000 people.  Their extensive meeting facilities include two well-appointed conference centers, a ballroom, numerous boardrooms, breathtaking outdoor spaces, the new Marina Riverside Center restaurants and bars, villa homes, a 6,000-seat amphitheater, and including three championship golf courses designed by one of the all-time greats, Pete Dye.

Let your Uniglobe Travel Advisor help you pick the perfect meeting venue at Casa de Campo to suit your group requirements.   Out of the box group activities can be planned to enhance your meeting. All this, combined with friendly staff, state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, exquisite food and drink menus, incentive programs and personalized service, will take your event to a whole new level of greatness.

Incentive Programs are Back

As incentive travel planning moves forward to 2023 and beyond, one thing remains constant, creative programs need to “wow” attendees with a meaningful experience. Taking your group to Casa de Campo will do just that!

Customized and unique experiences are plentiful at Casa de Campo for incentive groups.  From competitions on the famed Teeth of the Dog golf course, to a friendly donkey polo tournament, pottery classes or kayaking on the Chavon River, the great outdoors awaits your group. 

Call your Uniglobe Travel Advisor to find out how you can take your meeting, conference or incentive group to Paradise at Casa de Campo!

Nexus Update

For those clients who need to renew their Nexus card, we are pleased to inform you that NEXUS interviews have resumed at enrolment centres in the United States on April 19, 2022.  Canadian applicants wishing to schedule interviews can now do so at their nearest US enrolment centre through the Trusted Traveller Programs portal.   NEXUS enrolment centres in Canada continue to be closed.

Existing NEXUS members should renew their membership online before the expiry date to retain their membership privileges.  Your membership is valid until the expiry date indicated on your NEXUS card.

Please note that you need to be fully vaccinated to enter the US.  Prior to returning to Canada, you must submit your information into ArriveCAN, including your proof of vaccination. 

Call your Uniglobe Travel Advisor for all the current entry requirements for the US and Canada whether you are driving or flying.
