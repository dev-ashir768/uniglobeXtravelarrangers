      Negative Antigen Test Results to Be Accepted for Entry into Canada

      Starting February 28, testing options are expanding for travel

      All passengers over the age of 5 can provide a negative rapid antigen test result for travel to Canada. The antigen test must be taken no more than 1 day prior to departure, but results can be delivered  in as little as 15 minutes.

      Passengers can continue to present a negative molecular test, taken no more than 72 hours prior to departure, or provide proof of a positive molecular test result taken at least 10 days and no more than 180 days prior to entry. 

      Additionally, arrival testing will no longer be a mandatory requirement for fully vaccinated passengers, but rather they may be selected for random testing. Passengers selected for random testing will also no longer be required to quarantine while awaiting their test results.

      All passengers (regardless of how long they were away from Canada) will continue to be required to submit their mandatory information on the  ArriveCAN App.

      Connecting in Canada?

      If you 're a fully vaccinated Canadian or a foreign national:
      Regardless of vaccination status, if you have a domestic connection, you can complete your journey and take the test and quarantine at your final destination.

      If you are unvaccinated Canadian: 
      You are not permitted to continue onward domestically by air. Unvaccinated Canadians will continue to be tested on arrival, on Day 8, and must quarantine for 14 days. Border Services Officers and public health officials will provide direction to each traveller upon entry to Canada.

      Exemptions:
      There are limited exemptions (e.g., crew members, essential travellers) from the pre-departure test requirement. More information can be found on the  Government of Canada 's website.

      Welcome To WestJet Rewards

      Whether you travel every week or a few times a year, you are rewarded with WestJet. Their entry level, Teal, starts with your first dollar spent and you can earn your way up to Platinum.  

      Qualifying spends for each level are:

      • Teal - up to $2,999
      • Silver - $3,000 - $4,999
      • Gold - $5,000 - $7,999
      • Platinum - $8000 and beyond

      Each level has its perks, from savings on Westjet flights and vacation packages, car rentals, hotel stays, attractions and transfers. 

      Other flight benefits (depending on tier) include:

      • Free checked bags
      • Advance seat selection
      • Airport lounge access
      • Boarding privileges
      • Priority check-in
      • Priority baggage
      • No fee for same day flight changes
      • Complimentary upgrades prior to check-in

      If you are already a WestJet member, ensure your Uniglobe Travel Advisor has your membership number on file for automatic inclusion on all your bookings. If not, ask your Uniglobe Advisor how to become a member and start earning now.

       

      Exceptional Care for Exceptional Times.

      The world is now a different place. What you need from us has changed too.

      As with so many aspects of everyday life, the world of travel is undergoing major change due to the global COVID-19 pandemic. In addition to new policies and procedures while travelling, we can expect new and unique requirements and restrictions on travellers as well as other changes coming that are yet to be considered. 

      We offer services and programs designed to protect you and your company such as Uniglobe 7*24 Rescue Line, Uniglobe TripCheck and our Duty of Care provisions for safety, security and tracking of travellers. But our concept of Exceptional Care is even more than those.

      Your partner every step of the way.

      We are going to be your partner every step of the way as we emerge from this pandemic.

      This includes consulting on your travel program helping you make the right updates for your company and your traveller while being your “go-to ”source for up-to-date information and resources. We can help you navigate a world that will include frequently changing booking requirements and updated travel restrictions. Throughout, we will be your advocate with travel suppliers and vendors. We ’ll be here with you sharing our expertise, troubleshooting any issues you face, and getting you back to your regular schedule of travel.

      Questions to ask yourself to cover all your current travel policy and traveller safety requirements.

      Does your policy cover everyone ’s needs?

      Do you require an approval process or add another layer to the process?

      Can your travellers decline a request to travel for health concerns or personal reasons?

      Are your travellers well informed about the destination ’s status?

      Who should determine if a traveller will be allowed to travel armed with quarantine rules and immigration requirements for the destination?

      What is the minimum level of hygiene a supplier is required to perform at the time of reservation?

      What is the protocol should a traveller fall ill or have other concerns while travelling?

      Should the traveller sign a waiver if the supplier is outside of the company ’s travel policy?

      What apps do you require travellers to use in the destination?

      Does the company need to review our suite of  corporate offerings?

      Do you have travellers that book outside of your preferred Online Booking Tool or Travel Management Company? Use this time to reel them back in.

      Is it time to redefine the language in your travel policy?

      Do you have an executive list and their travel policy written?

      Do you allow your travel to add a leisure itinerary to their business itinerary? What sort of policy or waiver is required of them?
