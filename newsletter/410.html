      Air Canada Updates

      Travel Ready Hub is a guide to help you understand key travel requirements. However, it does not replace the information provided by government and other authorities. While Air Canada makes best efforts to keep this information updated, it is your responsibility to ensure that you meet all entry or transit requirements for each country on your itinerary.

      Air Canada Travel Ready Hub

       Automated carry-on baggage sizers

      To ensure that your carry-on baggage will fit onboard, Air Canada has  introduced automated carry-on baggage sizers pre-security in the Montreal (YUL), Toronto (YYZ), and Vancouver (YVR) airports.

      These new, touchless machines are easier and faster to use than the metal sizers. Please make sure that your carry-on bag  complies with the sizing  to avoid having to check your carry-on bag at the airport. Note that any non-compliant baggage will be assessed and is subject to applicable baggage fees.

      Flights between Ottawa and Toronto Island

      New easy and convenient flights between Ottawa, the nation ’s capital, and Toronto ’s downtown business centre begin October 31, 2021. Save time and additional travel by flying directly between Ottawa and Toronto Billy Bishop Airport with 4 daily flights, increasing to 8 daily in 2022.

      This new route is in addition to Air Canada 's  existing 5 daily non-stop flights between Montreal and Billy Bishop Airport.

      Looking for meeting space or just to get away?

      The JW Marriott Guanacaste resort in Costa Rica has 310 deluxe rooms and suites which offer  an unbeatable view  overlooking the Pacific Ocean. The resort offers 5 restaurants and 3 bars as well as a magnificent  outdoor swimming  pool, private beach, world class gym, biking and jogging trails, 18-hole golf course with a coastal layout and one of top five spas in Latin America as ranked by Spa Magazine. JW Marriott Guanacaste was named Top Resort Hotel in Central America in @travelLeisure 2021 World ’s Best Awards

      Meeting Facilities are also available at the resort including a grand ballroom. 10,000 square feet of indoor meeting space will cover all your event needs. With 4 break-out rooms and 8  outdoor private function areas, including terraces, pool gardens and Mansita Beach, the opportunities  are limitless. 

      Currently you do not need a negative Covid-19 test upon arrival in Costa Rica, just proof of double vaccination.  A negative test for your return to Canada is required 72 hours prior to your departure as per government regulations.  JW Marriott Guanacaste is able to connect you with the local hospital, clinic or labs  in  Guanacaste which offers PCR testing and are happy to help book an appointment and arrange transportation to the appointment. They  also have the option of In Hotel Testing available. As requirements can vary as to time needed for test prior to re-entry and/or specific test types, we recommend guests  review the requirements of their country of origin or final destination. 

      Contact your Uniglobe Travel Advisor and learn about the All-Inclusive Canadian dollar offer at the JW Guanacaste from Uniglobe Travel and WestJet Vacations.  Get away for business, pleasure or bleisure!
