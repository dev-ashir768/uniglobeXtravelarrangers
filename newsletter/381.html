      "Navigating The New Normal "- Why  You Need a Travel Management Company like Uniglobe Travel

      Whether you are a small business  or a  global conglomerate, the corporate travel landscape has never looked so foreign. In fact, an astounding  96 percent of travel managers surveyed by SAP Concur  felt that their companies were not adequately prepared to tackle the travel problems posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

      To successfully navigate this new terrain of unexplored opportunities and challenging pitfalls, businesses must first find the right tools and strategic partners for support. Uniglobe Travel can provide both of these: innovative tools and expert partnership. Below we ’ve outlined a few key reasons why  working with us  is critical to the recovery of your travel program and to helping you meet your business goals.

      Mitigate Risk and Keep Travellers Safe

      The biggest concern to the future of corporate travel is  to ensure the safety and wellness of employees.  The corporate traveller must feel safe as they return to the road. You must build Trust and Confidence in your travel program or its potential recovery will be significantly compromised.

      Uniglobe provides essential tools for your company ’s compliance with duty of care responsibilities. Manage your company ’s liability by insisting travellers book through the Uniglobe Travel program online in a controlled and safe environment. 

      Uniglobe Travel can help you manage risks and support your program with end-to-end processes to ensure traveller safety. Including:

      • E-travel Advisories
      • E-travel tracker dashboard
      • E-travel tracker map
      • E-travel reports and traveller contact details

      Uniglobe Travel has also created easy to access Covid-19 Information pages on our website that guides employees on the road through the new travel journey and outlines the most recent requirements and restrictions. 

      Manage Traveller Experience

      Establishing clear safety parameters provides a strong base for creating travel experiences that meet the needs and expectations of each and every traveller. Uniglobe Travel can provide a consistent but flexible approach to creating a smooth and comprehensive experience.

      We  focus on delivering exceptional service and engaging travellers using a range of technologies and methods including:

      • Multichannel reservations  and online technologies, including personal traveller advisor support
      • Automated unused ticket management
      • 24/7 traveller support
      • Quality Control on all reservations
      • Flight tracking including pre-departure email confirming flight status or delays
      • E-itineraries  

      Increase Efficiency through Technology

      Today ’s travel managers will be tasked to do more with less. Many organizations now have smaller support teams or reduced hours while facing added travel restrictions and advisories to evaluate. This is a daunting reality to consider, but investing in the right tools and technology can help streamline workflow.

      Let Uniglobe Travel assess your current  tools and recommend services you will need to meet the expectations of the new normal. 

      Leverage Industry Experience

      For small to mid-sized businesses in particular, it is important to continue your partnership with Uniglobe Travel as we can  provide leverage for your travel program. This may come in the form of added savings, industry connections, or rewards programs.

      Uniglobe  Travel holds a strong market position in the corporate space which affords  us leverage with travel suppliers.. We ’ve got you covered with:

      • Savings on air travel, lowest fare guarantees, fare match, status match, private fares through our Uniglobe Network
      • Corporate rates and complimentary  amenities at  hotels
      • Preferred rates and reward programs with our  car rental partners
      • Discounted airport parking
      • Discounted airport lounge access

      With the shifting travel landscape still evolving ahead, Uniglobe Travel can deliver continuity, strategy, and ROI for your business. From consultation and account management to robust services and technology solutions, 

      Let us show you the value Uniglobe Travel can bring to your corporate travel program —contact us to help you update or create your travel policy
