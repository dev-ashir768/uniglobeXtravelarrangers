The Air Canada App

Let the Air Canada App make travel better. Jump on board and enjoy all the advantages. The app is available on Apple iOS and Android devices.

With the Air Canada app, you can:

  • Stay up to date and get by-the-minute notifications on flight status, gate changes and boarding times
  • Access your boarding pass throughout the app for easy access. It is also saved for offline use and can even be added to your Wallet app.
  • Access your Aeroplan account and check your balance, track your Status, manage your account, and explore offers with our partners, all in one place! This is your go-to app for everything Aeroplan
  • Access maps of our most popular airports including Toronto (YYZ), Vancouver (YVR), Chicago (ORD), and Dubai (DXB) to find your gate, nearby lounges, and so much more with a tap of your finger
  • Track checked baggage and mobility aids while travelling in Canada, and to and from destinations across the U.S.

 

Extended Comfort

Extended Comfort on WestJet includes benefits that enhance your journey from start to finish. With perks like extra legroom and early access to overhead bin space, you'll experience additional comfort and convenience when you travel.

Extended Comfort is available for all guests who purchased an Economy fare (including UltraBasic, Econo, EconoFlex). Guests can select an Extended Comfort seat at the time of booking, after booking through Manage Trips and the WestJet App, or at check-in.

Benefits include:

Extra Leg room

Early access to overhead bins

Priority Boarding (zone 2)

Quicker exit upon arrival

Access to priority security lanes at select airports*

Ask your Uniglobe Travel Advisor to book your Extended Comfort seat on your next WestJet flight!

 

 

The Porter Experience

No Middle Seats

Porter offers choices when it comes to seat selection. And good news! There’s no middle seat, just an aisle or window.


PorterReserve seat included with PorterReserve fares

Front of cabin seating, 36” of legroom, early boarding and deplaning, complimentary premium beer, wine and snacks, complimentary fresh meals*, in-seat power**

PorterClassic stretch seat (not available on the Dash 8-400)

34” of legroom, complimentary premium beer, wine and snacks, in-seat power outlets or  PorterClassic  - 30” of legroom, complimentary premium beer, wine and snacks, in-seat power outlet**

*Fresh meals are available on flights over 2.5 hours on the E195-E2 jets only      **In-seat power is available on the E195-E2 jets only

Tasty Bites and Premium Sips

On every flight, Porter offers complimentary premium snacks, and a choice of beverages including free beer and wine served in real glassware. We also have delicious fresh food options available on flights over 2.5 hours aboard our E195-E2 jets. With our complimentary drinks and snacks, you’ll actually enjoy Economy when you fly Porter.

Free, Fast WiFi

Onboard our E195-E2 jets you can enjoy free, fast WiFi from any personal device from the moment you board until the moment you leave the aircraft. Powered by Viasat, no app download or registration is required. Passengers can view an ad to access 30 minutes of free WiFi-access or they can login to their VIPorter Account to receive uninterrupted access for the remainder of their flight.

Try out the Porter experience and see for yourself!
