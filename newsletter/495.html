      Free texting for Aeroplan Members  

      Sponsored by Bell

      Message your friends, family, and colleagues over Wi-Fi, while you fly! To start chatting, simply  connect to the onboard Wi-Fi  after take-off, select the "Free Texting "option, enter your last name and seat number and enjoy!

      To use free texting, make sure your  Aeroplan number is added to your booking before you complete your check-in. You can use: Apple ’s iMessage, Meta ’s Messenger and WhatsApp, Messages by Google and Rakuten ’s Viber. Please note that photos, videos and attachments are not supported.

      Conditions apply

       

      Vancouver to Singapore coming in 2024

      Air Canada is expanding its international network with the addition of non-stop flights between its trans-Pacific hub at  Vancouver  International Airport (YVR) and  Singapore ’s  Changi International Airport (SIN).

      The new route will operate four times weekly beginning  April 3, 2024*, onboard Air Canada ’s flagship Dreamliner fleet. Seats are available for purchase now with your Uniglobe Travel Advisor.

      Flights will be operated with Boeing 787 Dreamliner aircraft featuring three cabins of service for customers to choose from, including Signature Class with lie-flat seats, Premium Economy and Economy Class.

      The schedule has been built to conveniently connect to and from destinations across North America  via Air Canada ’s hub in Vancouver  and, in  Singapore  to and from destinations across  Southeast Asia, South India  & Western Australia  with  Star Alliance  and codeshare partner Singapore Airlines.

      *subject to final government approval

       

      A Perfect Meeting Space For Every Occasion in the Caribbean

      Hosting your next corporate event, conference or team-building activity at Casa de Campo will be completely worth the time for everyone involved.

      Make paradise the location of your next event and experience luxury in the Caribbean while accomplishing team goals. Casa de Campo offers a wide variety of scenarios to accommodate groups from 10 to 6,000 people. Their extensive meeting facilities include two well-appointed conference centers, a ballroom, numerous boardrooms, breathtaking outdoor spaces, the  Marina Riverside Center restaurants and bars, villa homes, a 6,000-seat amphitheater, and including three championship golf courses designed by one of the all-time greats, Pete Dye.

      Let your Uniglobe Travel Advisor help you pick the perfect meeting venue at Casa de Campo to suit your group 's requirements.  Out of the box group activities can be planned to enhance your meeting. All this, combined with friendly staff, state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, exquisite food and drink menus, incentive programs and personalized service, will take your event to a whole new level of greatness.

      Incentive Programs are Back

      As incentive travel planning moves forward to 2024  and beyond, one thing remains constant, creative programs need to “wow ”attendees with a meaningful experience. Taking your group to Casa de Campo will do just that!

      Customized and unique experiences are plentiful at Casa de Campo for incentive groups. From competitions on the famed Teeth of the Dog golf course, to a friendly donkey polo tournament, pottery classes or kayaking on the Chavon River, the great outdoors awaits your group.
